Computer Science and Business Systems Home Academics Programmes Offered Computer Science and Business Systems Department of Computer Science and Business Systems About Department OBE Job Profiles Academics OVERVIEW The B.Tech in Computer Science and Business System (CSBS) is a specialized undergraduate engineering program designed to blend core computer science education with insights into business systems and management. It is a relatively new course introduced in collaboration with industry leaders like Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) to bridge the gap between traditional computer science education and the dynamic needs of the IT and business world. This program aims to enhance the relevance of the Computer Science program in India to meet the future demands of the IT industry whose landscape is rapidly changing in the era of Business 4.0. The curriculum is designed to ensure that students should get better exposure to emerging subjects such as Analytics, Deep learning, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Business Analytics, Financial Management, Economics and Internet of Things and Design thinking and also to develop an equal interest and appreciation towards humanity, value added science and other interrelated topics. The students graduating from this program will be in high demand by the industries and will be productive. Objective: To produce graduates who are not only technically sound in computer science but also understand the fundamentals of business, economics, and management. Key Features Balanced Curriculum: Core Computer Science subjects (Data Structures, Algorithms, OS, DBMS, AI/ML, etc.) Business and Management subjects (Economics, Financial Management, Business Process Management, etc.) Industry Collaboration: Designed in partnership with TCS Emphasis on employability and industry readiness Focus Areas: Problem-solving using computational thinking Business analytics and decision-making IT systems for business applications Soft Skills Training: Communication Teamwork Entrepreneurial thinking Top Recruiters TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, CTS. Startups and Fin Tech companies like FSS. Benefits Over Regular CSE More interdisciplinary exposure Better suited for roles in tech-enabled business functions Greater alignment with industry expectations, especially in IT services and consulting. PROGRAMME B.Tech. - Computer Science and Business Systems Duration: 4 years (Regular) / 3 years (Lateral Entry) No. of Semesters: 8 (Regular) / 6 (Lateral Entry) Intake / No. of Seats: Total - 30 (Government - 15, Management - 15) Eligibility: 10+2 system of Education.
