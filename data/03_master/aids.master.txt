B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
Artificial intelligence (AI) is the process of creating machines to think like humans.
is a term used to describe the simulation of human intelligence processes in machines. It typically
involves sophisticated algorithms that enable computers to perform tasks that normally require human
intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition and decision making etc,.
Data Science is a field of knowledge which applies advanced statistical and
mathematical techniques to large datasets in order to extract knowledge from them. This data can be structured
like a spreadsheet but can also be unstructured like text or video data. Data science is the application of
techniques from statistics, mathematics, computer programming and other fields to solve real-world problems
Due to the exploration of the Internet, AI and DS deliver data-driven solutions using
computational principles, methods, and systems for extracting information from data and modern computational
systems that reveal capabilities of insight, way of thinking, learning, and action that are typical of human intelligence.

Problem Analysis:Identify, formulate, review research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences.
Design/Development Of Solutions: Design solutions for complex engineering problems and design system components or processes that meet the specified needs with appropriate consideration for the public health and safety, and the cultural, societal, and environmental considerations.
Conduct Investigations of Complex Problems:Use research-based knowledge and research methods including design of experiments, analysis and interpretation of data, and synthesis of the information to provide valid conclusions.
Modern Tool Usage:Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools including prediction and modeling to complex engineering activities with an understanding of the limitations.
The Engineer and Society: Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal and cultural issues and the consequent responsibilities relevant to the professional engineering practice.
Environment and Sustain ability: Understand the impact of the professional engineering solutions in societal and environmental contexts, and demonstrate the knowledge of, and need for sustainable development.
Ethics: Apply ethical principles and commit to professional ethics and responsibilities and norms of the engineering practice.
Individual and Team Work: Function effectively as an individual, and as a member or leader in diverse teams, and in multi disciplinary settings.
Communication: Communicate effectively on complex engineering activities with the engineering community and with society at large, such as, being able to comprehend and write effective reports and design documentation, make effective presentations, and give and receive clear instructions.
Project Management and Finance: Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the engineering and management principles and apply these to one's own work, as a member and leader in a team, to manage projects and in multi disciplinary environments.
Life-long Learning: Recognize the need for, and have the preparation and ability to engage in independent and life-long learning in the broadest context of technological change.
AI and Data Science Growth in India by 2025 – 2030 in India
The Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science sectors in India are having an
explosive growth over the next five years. AI-driven solutions and rely on data analytics to make better
decisions, the demand for professionals in these fields is expected to grow significantly. The employment
opportunity forecast for AI and Data Science in India from 2025 to 2030 is as follows.
Increased AI Adoption Across Industries:
Healthcare, Finance, E-commerce, Telecommunications, Automotive, and Manufacturing sectors.
AI will be used for tasks like Predictive analytics, Personalized services, Data-driven decision-making, and Automation.
AI-driven solutions in core business functions (HR, finance, customer service)
Government Support and Initiatives:
Digital India, National AI Strategy, and the National AI Mission initiatives boost AI research, startups, and adoption across public services.
Global leader in AI for sectors such as Smart cities, Healthcare, and Agriculture.
Rising Demand for Data-Driven Decision Making:
Customer segmentation, Fraud detection, and Supply chain optimization.
AI startups is expected to double in the next few years, creating job opportunities for AI engineers, data scientists, and product managers.
Integration of AI into Business Functions:
Industries like finance, retail, e-commerce, and manufacturing are increasingly adopting AI-based automation to streamline operations, reduce costs, and offer personalized experiences to customers.
Key AI and Data Science Job Roles in India (2025-2030)
Roles: Analyze large data sets to derive actionable insights, build predictive models, And help organizations make data-driven decisions.
Skills: Python, R, SQL, Machine Learning Algorithms, Statistical analysis, Data visualization (Tableau, Power BI), Deep learning.
Demand: The demand for data scientists in India will remain high, with industries across healthcare, finance, retail, and e-commerce
Roles: Build and deploy machine learning models, implement algorithms, and optimize the model performance for real-world applications.
Skills: Python, TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch, Neural networks, Cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud), Algorithm optimization.
Demand: As companies adopt more AI solutions, the demand for ML engineers will rise, especially in startups and tech giants.
Roles: Conduct cutting-edge research in AI, focus on Deep learning, Reinforcement learning, Computer vision, and Natural language processing (NLP).
Skills: Machine learning algorithms, Python, Mathematical modeling, Research publications, Deep learning frameworks.
Demand: With growing AI investment in India, especially from global tech companies and research labs, there will be a high demand for AI research scientists.
Roles: Collect, process, and analyze data to uncover trends, create dashboards, and support decision-making with data-driven insights.
Skills: Excel, SQL, Python, data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI), statistical tools (R, SAS).
Demand: As companies focus more on data-driven decision-making, the demand for data analysts in sectors like finance, retail, and healthcare will increase.
Roles: Design, construct, and maintain large-scale data pipelines, work with big data technologies to ensure data is stored, processed, and accessible.
Skills: SQL, Python, Hadoop, Spark, Kafka, cloud services (AWS, Google Cloud), ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes.
Demand: With the growth of big data and cloud computing, the demand for data engineers will continue to rise, especially in data-driven companies.
Roles: Oversee the development and deployment of AI-powered products, manage cross-functional teams of engineers and data scientists.
Skills: Product lifecycle management, agile methodology, machine learning, project management, business strategy.
Demand: The rise in AI product development across industries will create more opportunities for AI product managers.
Business Intelligence (BI) Developer:
Roles: Design and develop BI tools to collect, store, and analyze business data, help businesses make strategic decisions using AI-driven insights.
Skills: SQL, Power BI, Tableau, data visualization, reporting tools, Python.
Demand: As companies shift to data-driven strategies, the demand for BI developers will increase, particularly in e-commerce and retail.
Natural Language Processing (NLP) Engineer
Roles: Work on algorithms and models that allow machines to process and understand human language, like chatbots, virtual assistants, and sentiment analysis.
Skills: Python, NLP frameworks (NLTK, SpaCy, Hugging Face), machine learning, deep learning, text mining.
Demand: With the rise of AI chatbots, voice assistants, and text analytics, demand for NLP engineers will grow in e-commerce, finance, and telecommunications.
