[SECTION: Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering Overview]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is a premier educational institution located in Chennai, established with a vision to foster engineering excellence. The college offers degree programs in B.E, B.Tech, and M.E, and is affiliated with Anna University. MSAJCE emphasizes a holistic educational approach, integrating academic rigor with extracurricular activities to prepare students for real-world challenges.

[SECTION: Leadership and Faculty]
Dr. K.S. Srinivasan serves as the Principal of MSAJCE, leading the institution's commitment to academic excellence. He is supported by a dedicated faculty, including Dr. I. Manju, a Professor in the ECE department, and Dr. D. Weslin, an Associate Professor and the CSI Student Branch Counsellor. These leaders cultivate a supportive environment that encourages student engagement and professional development.

[SECTION: Professional Societies and Recognition]
MSAJCE hosts various professional societies, including the Computer Society of India (CSI), the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), and the Indian Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air Conditioning Engineers (ISHRAE). The college received recognition as the Best Supporting College by the CSI Kancheepuram Chapter, highlighting its commitment to industry collaboration and student support.

[SECTION: Student Leadership and Involvement]
The student leadership at MSAJCE is robust with key positions filled by notable individuals. Yogesh R is the President from the IT department for the 2022-2026 batch, while Saqlin Mustaq M serves as Vice President from the AI&DS department for the 2023-2027 batch. This dynamic leadership fosters a spirit of collaboration and innovation among students.

[SECTION: Workshops and Learning Opportunities]
MSAJCE organizes various workshops and lectures for skill enhancement. Noteworthy events include a guest lecture on "Voice over IP" by Mr. Anbazhagan A.T., and an interactive session on "Innovation & Invention in AI Technology" led by Ms. Anisha Udhayakumar. These activities provide students with exposure to current industry trends and technical advancements.

[SECTION: Engineering Competitions and Events]
The college actively participates in engineering competitions, such as the BAJA SAE India and SUPRA SAEINDIA events. These competitions are crucial for practical learning and industry exposure, allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world challenges while enhancing their teamwork and leadership skills.

[SECTION: ISHRAE Involvement and Activities]
The Indian Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air Conditioning Engineers (ISHRAE) at MSAJCE endorses professional development in HVAC&R. It organizes workshops, seminars, and networking opportunities, enabling students to connect with esteemed professionals and stay informed about industry standards and innovations.

[SECTION: Facilities and Campus Life]
The MSAJCE campus offers comprehensive facilities, including a well-equipped library, modern hostels, and various extracurricular amenities. The college promotes a vibrant campus life, allowing students to thrive academically while engaging in sports, clubs, and community service initiatives.
