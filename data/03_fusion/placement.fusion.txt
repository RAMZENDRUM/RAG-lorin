[SECTION: MSAJCE Placement Overview]
The Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) prioritizes enhancing employability for its students by providing comprehensive placement services and resources. The Placement Cell organizes on-campus and off-campus recruitment drives and offers specialized training in aptitude, communication, and technical skills to prepare graduates for the competitive job market. They also arrange internships, industrial visits, and workshops led by industry experts to bridge gaps between academic knowledge and practical application. Key personnel managing these initiatives include Mr. S.V. Vinodh, the Placement Officer, and Mr. Ajin Sijo John, the Assistant Placement Officer. 

[SECTION: Industry Institute Interaction Cell]
MSAJCE’s Industry Institute Interaction Cell facilitates collaboration between students and industry, addressing the crucial gap between classroom knowledge and real-world applications. The cell focuses on signing Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) with various industries to enhance the educational experience. These partnerships enable students to engage in internships and participate in value-added courses and workshops, thus ensuring they meet industry expectations and acquire relevant skills. The cell also invites industry experts for guest lectures, contributing to a well-rounded learning environment.

[SECTION: Training Programs and Workshops]
Throughout the academic years, MSAJCE hosts various training programs and workshops aimed at enhancing student skills. For instance, the Soft Skill Training Program, held every year, equips students with essential interpersonal skills to thrive in professional settings. In 2023, significant initiatives included a Placement Training on Aptitude Skills and multiple sessions focusing on programming, technical interviews, and soft skills. These workshops not only prepare students for immediate job placements but also encourage lifelong learning and adaptability.

[SECTION: Major Recruiters]
MSAJCE collaborates with numerous reputed companies to provide diverse employment opportunities for its graduates. Notable industry partners include Lenovo, Zoho Tech, and Green Valleys Shelters Pvt. Ltd., which have hired significant numbers of students. The institution’s proactive approach to engaging with industry leaders ensures graduates possess the necessary skills to contribute effectively from day one. The ongoing relationships with these companies also facilitate industry visits and talks, further enriching the educational experience.

[SECTION: Student Higher Education Details]
MSAJCE also emphasizes higher education pursuits among its graduates. A dedicated Higher Education Cell conducts regular counseling sessions and organizes seminars to inform students about potential paths for further studies. Recent graduates have headed to prestigious institutions such as Anna University and Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham to pursue specialized fields including Structural Engineering and Data Science. This initiative aims to maximize students' academic and career potential post-graduation.

[SECTION: Event Highlights]
Annual events such as the Graduation Day and various academic seminars focus on inspiring students and showcasing industry innovations. The 2025 Graduation Day, held on 22 February, celebrates student achievements and serves as a networking platform for graduates entering the workforce. Additionally, seminars on topics like Cyber Security and Data Analytics, led by industry experts, prepare students by keeping them updated with current trends and best practices.
