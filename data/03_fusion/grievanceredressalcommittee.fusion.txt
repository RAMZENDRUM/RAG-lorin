[SECTION: Grievance Redressal Committee]
The Grievance Redressal Committee at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) plays a vital role in addressing concerns from students and faculty. It is led by Ms. S. Usha, an Assistant Professor in English, serving as the Convener. The committee includes various members such as external advocates and representatives from administration, ensuring a comprehensive approach to managing grievances. The email for submissions is grievance@msajce-edu.in.

[SECTION: Committee Members]
The Grievance Redressal Committee consists of several key members: Ms. S. Usha (Convener), Principal (Co-Convener), and various external members including Mrs. Adhilakshmi Logamurthy, an Advocate, and Mr. Hussain Basha, an NGO Consultant. Other important roles include members from MSAJCE like the Heads of Academics and Administration, and student representatives from various departments, fostering an inclusive atmosphere for all stakeholders.

[SECTION: Objective and Activities]
The primary objective of the Grievance Redressal Committee at MSAJCE is to provide a transparent platform for promptly addressing and resolving grievances of students and faculty members. The committee undertakes activities such as reviewing complaints, organizing awareness sessions regarding grievance procedures, and ensuring compliance with institutional policies on student welfare.

[SECTION: Contact Information]
For any grievances or suggestions, individuals can directly contact the Grievance Redressal Committee at grievance@msajce-edu.in or reach the office by calling +91 99400 04500. The committee ensures that all communications are handled with confidentiality and respect, fostering a supportive environment at MSAJCE.
