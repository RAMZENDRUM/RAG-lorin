[SECTION: Academic Advisory Committee Overview]
The Academic Advisory Committee at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is led by Dr. K.S. Srinivasan, the Principal, serving as the Chairman. The committee comprises several advisors, including Dr. K. Malathi from Anna University and Dr. N. Parthiban from SRM University, emphasizing a blend of academic leadership and industry experience. This structure aims to bridge the gap between academic practices and industry needs, fostering better educational outcomes for students.
