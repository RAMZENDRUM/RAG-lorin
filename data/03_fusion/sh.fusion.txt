[SECTION: Overview of MSAJCE]
The Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) in Chennai is dedicated to cultivating proficient engineers through innovative and sustainable educational practices. Established in 2000, the college emphasizes a collaborative approach, engaging students in various extracurricular activities like NSS, YRC, and more, promoting both academic excellence and societal responsibility.

[SECTION: Program Information]
MSAJCE offers a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Mechanical Engineering, with a duration of 4 years for regular students and 3 years for lateral entry. The program includes eight semesters and has a total intake of 120 students—60 from government and 60 from management. Eligibility criteria require candidates to pass their 10+2 exams in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

[SECTION: Educational Objectives]
The Program Educational Objectives (PEOs) for mechanical engineering graduates at MSAJCE are threefold: first, to design innovative mechanical components while prioritizing sustainability; second, to equip students with modern problem-solving skills; and finally, to develop managerial and leadership capabilities essential for entrepreneurship. 

[SECTION: Specific Outcomes]
The Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs) focus on applying mechanical engineering knowledge to solve industrial problems and creating eco-friendly components for society. Graduates will be adept at using modern tools for critical analyses, thereby addressing industrial challenges effectively.

[SECTION: Graduate Outcomes]
Engineering graduates from MSAJCE are expected to demonstrate a comprehensive understanding of mathematics and science, conduct complex problem analyses, and design effective solutions that consider public health, safety, and environmental concerns. They will also embody ethical practices and effective communication skills in professional settings.

[SECTION: Faculty Profiles]
MSAJCE boasts a dedicated team of educators, including Dr. Ammu Qudsiya A., an Associate Professor with a Ph.D., who joined in December 2022, and several other qualified individuals, such as Mrs. Senthil Lakshmi and Dr. Kavitha J., both committed to fostering an enriching learning environment. 

[SECTION: Research and Facilities]
MSAJCE supports various research initiatives, equipped with advanced laboratories, including Physics and Chemistry labs for hands-on experience. The college enhances learning through innovative teaching methods, fostering critical thinking and creativity among students.
