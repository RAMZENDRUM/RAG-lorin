[SECTION: Overview of Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is a prominent engineering institution located in Chennai, India. Established with a commitment to academic excellence, it offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines such as Civil Engineering, Computer Science, and Mechanical Engineering. The college is recognized for its focus on research and development, providing a solid foundation for students aiming for successful careers in engineering and technology.

[SECTION: Academic Departments]
MSAJCE boasts a diverse range of academic departments, including Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical and Electronic Engineering, and many more. Specialized programs also include Artificial Intelligence and Data Science and Computer Science with Cyber Security. This extensive departmental structure ensures that students receive comprehensive education tailored to industry demands while promoting interdisciplinary collaboration.

[SECTION: Graduation Days and Events]
MSAJCE hosts significant events such as the Graduation Days, with the 18th and 17th ceremonies scheduled for February 22, 2025, celebrating the achievements of its graduates. Such events not only mark an important milestone in students' academic journeys but also encourage alumni engagement and community building within the college.

[SECTION: Admissions and Internships]
Admissions for the academic year 2026-2027 are currently open, with options for online application and fee payment available to prospective students. In addition, MSAJCE emphasizes practical experience through internships, providing valuable opportunities for students to enhance their skills and employability in a competitive job market.
