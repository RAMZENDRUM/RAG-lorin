[SECTION: Bentley’s Building Information Modelling Centre]
The Bentley’s Building Information Modelling (BIM) Centre at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering is designed to equip students with essential theoretical knowledge and practical skills in BIM techniques. Students will learn to interpret 2D and 3D drawings, understand the National Building Code regulations, and prepare for actual work situations through hands-on training. Key activities include creating building designs using AutoCAD and simulating virtual models with Revit Architecture, ultimately enhancing their structural analysis capabilities.

[SECTION: Cisco Networking Academy Partnership]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering has established a partnership with Cisco to launch the Networking Academy (NetAcad). This program provides students with unique hands-on learning experiences in networking, security, and cloud technologies using advanced simulation tools. It prepares learners for industry-recognized professional certifications, connecting them to an estimated eight million networking jobs worldwide. Courses offered include Network Essentials and CCNA certifications, enhancing employability in the tech sector.

[SECTION: Courses and Certifications Offered]
The college offers a diverse range of courses aimed at developing students' networking skills, including essentials and advanced CCNA tracks. Notable courses are CCNA: Introduction to Networks, Security Courses, and IoT & Data Analytics Courses, all designed to meet industry demands. Certifications like CISCO Certified Network Associate further enhance students' qualifications, empowering them for successful careers in the tech industry.

[SECTION: Practical Training Objectives]
Students in the BIM Centre will gain the ability to prepare components that meet real-world economic, environmental, social, ethical, and sustainability constraints. They will learn to design and analyze structures such as retaining walls and industrial components through hands-on practice with software like STAAD.Pro. This curriculum is aimed at bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in engineering roles.
