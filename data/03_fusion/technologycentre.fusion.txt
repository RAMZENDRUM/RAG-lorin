[SECTION: Technology Centres at MSAJCE]
The Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE), established in 2019-2020, features various Technology Centres to enhance students' skills in emerging industrial demands. These centres, such as the Centre for 3D Printing and Additive Manufacturing, Centre for Industrial Robotics, and Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, are designed based on AICTE recommendations. Collaborating with industry leaders like Automation Anywhere, Godrej Inc., and Amazon Web Services, these centres provide students with opportunities for hands-on projects, certifications, and internships. 

[SECTION: Purpose and Impact of Technology Centres]
MSAJCE’s Technology Centres aim to up-skill students by providing state-of-the-art facilities for research and innovation. These centres play a critical role in creating a conducive learning environment, empowering students to become deployable and proficient in key technology areas. By offering programs in fields like E-Mobility, Renewable Energy, and Drone Technology, the college ensures its graduates are equipped to meet industry requirements. This initiative aligns with the latest trends identified in the India Skill Report 2020.

[SECTION: List of Key Technology Centres]
The college boasts several specialized centres that cater to a diverse set of technological fields. Notable centres include the Centre for Bot Lab & Robotics Process Automation, Centre for Embedded & IoT, and Centre for UAV/Drone Technology. Each centre aims to provide students with advanced training and hands-on experience, bridging the gap between academic learning and practical application. By engaging with these centres, students not only learn essential skills but also enhance their employability in a competitive job market.

[SECTION: Industry Collaborations and Certifications]
MSAJCE’s Technology Centres collaborate with numerous well-known organizations to facilitate industry-relevant training programs. Partnerships with esteemed firms like Cisco Academy and Bosch Industry Institute allow students access to certifications and practical internships. These collaborations enrich the learning experience, ensuring students gain practical insights and valuable industry exposure that prepares them for future employment.
