[SECTION: Transport Facility Overview]
The Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) offers a comprehensive transport facility to assist students and staff in accessing the campus with ease. The college operates 22 buses, one Tata ACE, and one ambulance, providing daily transportation from various locations, including Chennai, Chengalpattu, and Kanchipuram. This transport system supports local social service, sports, and educational activities, ensuring timely arrival for all users.

[SECTION: Transport Committee Details]
The Transport Committee at MSAJCE oversees the smooth operation of the college bus services. Led by Dr. K. P. Santhosh Nathan, the Transport Convener, and assisted by Mr. A. Abdul Gafoor, the Assistant Transport Convener, this committee meets biannually to evaluate service performance and address operational concerns. The committee includes staff dedicated to ensuring the safe and efficient functioning of the transport system.

[SECTION: MTC Bus Routes]
MSAJCE provides various Metro Transport Corporation (MTC) bus routes to facilitate easy commutes. Key routes include Bus Route 19 from T. Nagar to Thiruporur via OMR, and Bus Route 519 also connecting T. Nagar with multiple daily services. These routes accommodate students traveling from key areas, enhancing access to the college.

[SECTION: Scheduled Bus Timings]
The college bus schedule includes early morning departures, such as Route AR 3 from Uthiramerur at 5:50 AM and Route R 22 from Nemilichery at 6:00 AM. These timely routes are designed to accommodate the academic schedules of students, ensuring reliable transportation from various starting points to MSAJCE.
