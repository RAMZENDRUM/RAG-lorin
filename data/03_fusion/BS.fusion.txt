[SECTION: College Overview]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE), located in Siruseri, Chennai, offers undergraduate (B.E, B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.E) programs. The college is recognized for its academic rigor and commitment to quality education, further endorsed by approvals from AICTE and Anna University. With a focus on innovation, MSAJCE promotes student engagement through various activities, including internships, graduation ceremonies, and faculty development programs.

[SECTION: Admission Process]
Admissions for the academic year 2026-2027 at MSAJCE are currently open. Prospective students can apply through the online application form available on the college website. The institution emphasizes a well-structured admission process aimed at nurturing future engineers, promoting a blend of academic excellence and professional readiness.

[SECTION: Facilities and Campus Life]
MSAJCE boasts a wide range of facilities including a comprehensive learning center (library), hostels, and transportation services. Students are encouraged to participate in extracurricular activities, sports, and social services, enriching their college experience. The lively campus environment is complemented by clubs and professional societies designed to foster teamwork and leadership skills among students.

[SECTION: Events and Celebrations]
The college celebrates significant events such as the Graduation Day, with the next ceremony scheduled for 22nd February 2025. These occasions not only showcase student achievements but also strengthen the community spirit among faculty, students, and parents. Additionally, the Teachers and Engineers Day celebrations highlight the contributions of staff and alumni in shaping the future of engineering.
