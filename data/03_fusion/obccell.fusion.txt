[SECTION: MSAJCE Overview]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is a premier engineering institution located in Chennai, India. Established with a vision to foster academic excellence, MSAJCE offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, accredited by AICTE. The college emphasizes holistic development through various student-oriented initiatives and activities, ensuring a vibrant campus life.

[SECTION: Governance and Leadership]
The MSAJCE governance structure includes multiple committees such as the Governing Council and the Academic Advisory Committee, ensuring effective oversight and strategic planning. The Principal leads these committees and is supported by key stakeholders including faculty and alumni. This collaborative leadership framework enhances the educational experience and upholds academic integrity.

[SECTION: Key Personnel]
Dr. E. Dhiravidachelvi, a Professor in the Electronics and Communication Engineering department, serves as the Convener of the OBC Cell at MSAJCE. Alongside him, other notable members include Dr. I. Manju, a Professor in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, and Mrs. Adhilakshmi Logamurthy, an Advocate and Legal Consultant, who contribute significantly to the college's governance and support services.

[SECTION: Admission and Academic Programs]
MSAJCE invites applications for various engineering programs for the academic year 2026-2027, with online application forms available on their official website. The college's curriculum is designed to meet both industry standards and academic rigor, fostering a solid foundation for engineering students. 

[SECTION: Events and Activities]
Major events at MSAJCE, such as the Graduation Day and various workshops like the AICTE-ATAL Sponsored Faculty Development Program, create opportunities for student engagement and professional development. The 18th Graduation Day is scheduled for February 22, 2025, celebrating the achievements of graduating students alongside faculty and industry leaders.
