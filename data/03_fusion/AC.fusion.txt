[SECTION: Overview of MSAJCE]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is located in Siruseri, Chennai. The college offers various degree programs including B.E, B.Tech, and M.E. MSAJCE is known for its commitment to quality education and innovative teaching methods, aiming to empower future engineers through a comprehensive curriculum and extracurricular activities. 

[SECTION: Admission Information]
The admissions for the academic year 2026-2027 are now open at MSAJCE, with an online application form available for prospective students. The institution supports applicants with a dedicated online fee payment system to streamline the admissions process. Interested candidates can also contact the college at +91 99400 04500 for further assistance.

[SECTION: Graduation Days Celebration]
MSAJCE proudly announced the celebrations of its 18th Graduation Day, scheduled for February 22, 2025. This event marks a significant milestone for graduating students and will feature various activities to honor their academic achievements. The college encourages family and friends to participate in this memorable occasion.

[SECTION: Committee Structure]
MSAJCE includes several committees to ensure effective governance and student welfare. Notable committees are the Grievance and Redressal Committee and the Anti-Ragging Committee, which aim to create a safe and supportive environment for students. Additionally, the Women's Empowerment Cell promotes gender equality and women's rights within the college.

[SECTION: Academic and Research Opportunities]
The college offers diverse academic programs and regularly updates its curriculum and syllabus to align with industry standards. MSAJCE also focuses on research and development through various initiatives, including AICTE scholarships and incubation programs, fostering innovation among students and faculty.
