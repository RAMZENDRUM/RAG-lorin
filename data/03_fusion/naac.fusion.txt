[SECTION: Curricular Aspects]
The Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) ensures effective curriculum delivery through a well-documented process and adherence to the academic calendar. It integrates key topics such as professional ethics and sustainability, with approximately 75% of its programs implementing the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS). Furthermore, the institution provides add-on and certificate programs, targeting an average enrollment rate of 10% in these initiatives over the past five years.

[SECTION: Teaching Learning and Evaluation]
MSAJCE maintains an average student enrollment percentage of 80% over the last five years, while also focusing on the student to teacher ratio at 15:1. The college employs student-centric methods including experiential learning and problem-solving techniques. Furthermore, its mechanism for internal assessments is robust, ensuring transparent evaluation and frequent feedback from students.

[SECTION: Research, Innovations and Extension]
The college encourages a culture of research with substantial funding received from government and non-governmental agencies. Approximately 20% of teachers are recognized as research guides, fostering an environment conducive to innovation. MSAJCE actively participates in community outreach programs, with a significant number of students engaging in initiatives such as Swachh Bharat and gender awareness activities over the last five years.

[SECTION: Infrastructure and Learning Resources]
MSAJCE boasts adequate physical facilities for teaching and learning, including well-equipped classrooms and laboratories. The institution maintains a balanced student-computer ratio of 5:1 and ensures library resources are automated with subscriptions to e-resources. Regular updates to IT facilities, such as Wi-Fi connectivity, enhance the learning experience for students.

[SECTION: Student Support and Progression]
The college prioritizes student support, with about 30% of students benefiting from government scholarships. It offers guidance for competitive examinations and career counseling, reflecting a transparent grievance redressal mechanism. Alumni contributions play a vital role in institutional growth, with financial support exceeding INR 10 lakhs in the last five years.

[SECTION: Governance, Leadership and Management]
Governance at MSAJCE aligns with its mission and vision, exhibiting effective leadership through decentralized and participative management strategies. The institution implements e-governance in operational areas, and there are regular financial audits to maintain transparency and accountability across all levels of administration.

[SECTION: Institutional Values and Best Practices]
MSAJCE promotes institutional values through initiatives that foster gender equity and environmental sustainability. It has established facilities for energy conservation and conducts regular quality audits. The institution's commitment to a disabled-friendly campus enhances inclusivity, while its code of conduct ensures that all members adhere to ethical standards.
