[SECTION: Overview of MSAJCE]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is located in Siruseri, Chennai, and offers various engineering programs including B.E, B.Tech, and M.E. The college is committed to academic excellence and provides facilities such as a learning center, hostel, and sports. The vision and mission emphasize quality education and student empowerment.

[SECTION: SC/ST Cell]
The SC/ST Cell at MSAJCE aims to promote awareness and provide support for students from scheduled castes and scheduled tribes. The cell is chaired by Dr. E. Dhiravidachelvi, a Professor in the ECE department, along with members from various departments and legal consultancy to address student grievances and promote inclusivity.

[SECTION: Admission Information]
MSAJCE is currently accepting online applications for the academic year 2026-2027. The college offers a user-friendly online application process and fee payment options. Prospective students can engage in various programs tailored to enhance their academic and professional skills.

[SECTION: Graduation Events]
MSAJCE holds significant graduation events annually, with the 18th Graduation Day scheduled on February 22, 2025, showcasing student achievements. The college celebrates milestones and academic excellence through organized ceremonies and various activities.

[SECTION: Faculty and Staff]
The faculty members at MSAJCE are experienced and diverse, including notable professors like Dr. E. Dhiravidachelvi, Dr. I. Manju, Mrs. Adhilakshmi Logamurthy, and many others contributing to both academic and extracurricular activities. Their expertise spans various engineering disciplines, ensuring comprehensive education for students.
