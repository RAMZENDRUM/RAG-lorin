[SECTION: MSAJCE Alumni Association Overview]
The MSAJCE Alumni Association aims to reconnect alumni with Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering, fostering enduring relationships and support for future engineers. Established to enhance collaboration between the alumni community and the institution, the association engages alumni in various activities that contribute to the college’s growth and student welfare. Notably, they provide financial assistance through scholarships, amounting to over Rs. 37,94,972, aimed at underprivileged students. Keywords: MSAJCE, Alumni Association, student support, scholarships.

The current leadership of the MSAJCE Alumni Association includes Dr. K. S. Srinivasan as President, Mr. Ahamed Jameel A.M.T as Secretary, and Dr. S. Vijayakumar as Treasurer.
