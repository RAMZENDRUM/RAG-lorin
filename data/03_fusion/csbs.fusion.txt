### [SECTION: Computer Science and Business Systems Overview]
The Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS) at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering is a four-year program designed to integrate core computer science education with business management principles. This innovative curriculum, introduced in collaboration with industry leaders such as Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), aims to align with the fast-evolving demands of the IT and business sectors. It encompasses a range of subjects including deep learning, machine learning, cloud computing, and business analytics to ensure graduates are industry-ready.

### [SECTION: Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)]
The Computer Science and Business Systems program aims to achieve key educational objectives: graduates will be equipped to analyze, design, develop, and test software solutions creatively (PEO1); utilize modern tools for problem-solving in industrial contexts (PEO2); and gain managerial skills suitable for career advancement or entrepreneurship (PEO3). These objectives prepare students to tackle challenges in a rapidly digitalizing world.

### [SECTION: Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)]
Students pursuing the CSBS program will achieve significant program-specific outcomes: the ability to creatively apply emerging technologies to real-time applications (PSO1) and develop software and mobile applications for various automation needs (PSO2). These outcomes reflect the skills necessary for innovation and practical application in the tech industry.

### [SECTION: Key Features of the Curriculum]
The CSBS curriculum features a balanced educational approach, incorporating core computer science subjects such as Data Structures and Algorithms, alongside business management topics like Economics and Financial Management. This interdisciplinary exposure enhances students' employability and better prepares them for roles that merge technology and business strategies in various industries.

### [SECTION: Career Opportunities and Employment Growth]
From 2025 to 2030, the demand for professionals in Computer Science and Business Systems is projected to grow significantly due to increased digitalization, e-commerce expansion, and advances in AI technology. Graduates will find opportunities in diverse sectors such as FinTech and healthcare, where there is a pressing need for individuals capable of bridging technical skills with business acumen.

### [SECTION: Faculty Expertise]
The program is supported by a team of experienced faculty members, including Dr. Nagasubramanian R (Head - PG, Computer Science Engineering) and Dr. Subramanian P (Head - UG, Wireless Sensor Networking and Image Processing). Their expertise across various specialized fields ensures high-quality education and guidance for students throughout their academic journey.

### [SECTION: Admission Information]
The CSBS program is open for enrollment with an intake of 30 students (15 for Government and 15 for Management). Eligibility requires passing the 10+2 system of education with a focus on Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. This rigorous selection fosters a foundational knowledge essential for success in advanced studies and professional practice.

### [SECTION: Professional Development and Soft Skills Training]
In addition to technical education, the curriculum emphasizes the development of soft skills, including teamwork, communication, and entrepreneurial thinking. This comprehensive approach prepares students not only for immediate career challenges but also equips them with lifelong learning capabilities that are crucial in the modern workforce.
