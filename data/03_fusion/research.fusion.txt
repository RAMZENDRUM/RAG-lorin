### [SECTION: Research and Development at MSAJCE]
The Research and Development Cell at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) promotes research in Engineering, Technology, Science, and Humanities. Its mission includes fostering innovation, cultivating publication zeal among students and faculty, and facilitating collaborations with industries to enhance productivity. The cell organizes workshops and assists students in securing funding from agencies like TNSCST and MSME, thereby supporting a robust research culture and facilitating the creation of patents and copyrights.

### [SECTION: Patents Filed by MSAJCE Faculty]
Several patents have been filed by faculty at MSAJCE, showcasing innovative solutions in various fields. Examples include Dr. E. Dhiravidachelvi's disaster management system using IoT, and Mr. K. Vairaperumal's station for battery swapping in future electric vehicle transportation. These patents illustrate the institution's commitment to advancing technology and research, reflecting the dedication of its faculty in pushing boundaries within their disciplines.

### [SECTION: Copyrights Registered at MSAJCE]
MSAJCE has registered multiple copyrights emphasizing its focus on educational resources. Notable works include "Computer Aided Design - Course Manual" by Dr. R. Senthil Kumar and "Engineering Practices Lab" by Dr. R. Senthilkumar and G. Ramesh. Such copyrights contribute to creating quality learning materials and underscore the institute's drive to enhance academic excellence and pedagogical practices.

### [SECTION: National Innovation and Start-up Policy (NISP)]
Under the NISP, MSAJCE aims to nurture a culture of entrepreneurship among students through various initiatives. The policy focuses on developing infrastructure to support innovative ideas and facilitating industry-academic partnerships. It seeks to engage students in realizing their entrepreneurship potential, ultimately contributing to national economic growth by fostering innovation and start-up ecosystems within the institutional framework. 

### [SECTION: KPIs for Entrepreneurship Development]
The Entrepreneurship Development Cell at MSAJCE has established key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure success. Goals include creating five start-ups annually, ensuring 25% of students participate in entrepreneurship orientation programs, and achieving significant industry collaboration. These metrics enable the institute to track progress in enhancing entrepreneurial skills and market readiness among students, fostering a more entrepreneurial environment.

### [SECTION: Activities Promoting Innovation and Start-up Culture]
MSAJCE organizes diverse events to promote innovation among students and faculty. Activities include workshops on problem-solving and ideation, internships during semester breaks, and seminars on venture capital funding. These initiatives are designed to provide practical exposure, mentorship opportunities, and essential skills, aligning with the institution's vision of fostering a vibrant start-up culture.

### [SECTION: List of Committee Members for Research and Innovation]

This committee plays a pivotal role in driving research initiatives and ensuring that the objectives of the Research and Development Cell are met, thereby fostering a collaborative and innovative academic environment.

### [SECTION: Achievements of MSAJCE in Publications]
Throughout recent years, faculty and students at MSAJCE have published significant research across various disciplines. This commitment to scholarly output enhances the academic reputation of the institution and showcases the collective expertise and innovative research contributions of its academic community, positioning MSAJCE as a prominent player in academic research.

### [SECTION: Future Plans for Incubation and Innovation]
MSAJCE plans to expand its incubation support with a focus on developing organizational capacity for fostering entrepreneurship.
