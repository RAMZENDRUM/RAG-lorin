[SECTION: Coding Learning Centre Objectives]
The Coding Learning Centre at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering focuses on equipping students with essential programming skills applicable across various domains. Key objectives include understanding programming languages such as C, C++, Java, and Python, and fostering a new problem-solving perspective. Students are prepared as competent data analysts through courses on Big Data and Hadoop, and they enhance their software testing abilities by learning to utilize tools like Selenium. Participation in global coding contests promotes competitive learning among students.

[SECTION: Networking Academy Partnership]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering has established a strategic partnership with CISCO to launch the Networking Academy. This IT skills program provides students with a unique hands-on learning experience in modern networking, security, and cloud technologies through simulation tools. It offers professional certifications recognized globally, creating career pathways to over eight million networking jobs. The academy emphasizes critical courses including Network Essentials, CCNA, and more, positioning students for successful careers in technology. 

[SECTION: Courses Offered at the Technology Centre]
The Technology Centre at MSAJCE provides a rich array of courses covering essential and advanced topics in networking and software development. Offered courses include Network Essentials, Mobility Fundamentals, and various CCNA tracks. Additionally, students can engage in cybersecurity, IoT, data analytics, and programming courses tailored for both industry readiness and entrepreneurship. This diverse curriculum ensures that students are well-equipped to meet the evolving demands of the tech sector.

[SECTION: Professional Certifications Available]
The Coding Learning Centre offers professional certifications implemented by CISCO, including the CISCO Certified Network Associate and Implementing CISCO Enterprise Network Core Technologies. These certifications are highly valued in the job market, ensuring that students gain recognition from employers worldwide. Completing these certification programs not only enhances students' resumes but also prepares them for impactful careers in information technology and networking.
