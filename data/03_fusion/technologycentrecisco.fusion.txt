[SECTION: MSAJCE Networking Academy]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering has established a partnership with Cisco to create the Networking Academy, an IT skills and career-building program. The academy offers unique hands-on experiences in networking, security, and cloud technologies, complemented by a comprehensive curriculum that includes 24 labs utilizing Cisco 4221 routers and 2960 switches. This program is designed to connect students with professional certifications, recognized by employers globally, paving the way for career opportunities in over eight million networking jobs worldwide.

[SECTION: Networking Course Offerings]
The Cisco Networking Academy at MSAJCE provides a diverse range of courses, including Network Essentials, CCNA: Introduction to Networks, and IoT & Data Analytics Courses. With a structured approach, students learn foundational and advanced networking concepts such as Ethernet configuration, IP addressing, and network security fundamentals. The training emphasizes real-world applications to ensure students are job-ready.

[SECTION: Hands-On Learning Approach]
The Networking Academy utilizes a simulated “model Internet” and the Packet Tracer tool to empower students with a practical understanding of network configurations. Students engage in building Local Area Networks (LANs), configuring routers and switches, and resolving issues within both IPv4 and IPv6 environments. This hands-on methodology is essential for developing essential troubleshooting skills and networking knowledge.

[SECTION: Course Certification and Skills Development]
Upon completion of the training at MSAJCE's Networking Academy, students can earn the Cisco Certified Network Associate certification. This credential signifies proficiency in implementing and troubleshooting enterprise network core technologies, crucial for career advancement in the IT industry. The curriculum's design encourages critical thinking and practical application of networking concepts.

[SECTION: Program Outcomes]
The Cisco Networking Academy program equips students with the ability to build robust networks and implement complex designs necessary for modern applications. Key outcomes include acquiring skills in network design, security, and troubleshooting methodologies, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for the dynamic challenges in the tech industry.
