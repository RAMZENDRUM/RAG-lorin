[SECTION: College Overview]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is a top-tier engineering institution in Chennai, offering Bachelor's and Master's programs in various fields of engineering. Founded with a vision to empower students through knowledge and innovation, MSAJCE aims to equip graduates with the skills needed to excel in competitive environments. The college is affiliated with Anna University and is approved by AICTE.

[SECTION: Events and Celebrations]
MSAJCE hosts numerous events to enrich student experience and community engagement. Major upcoming events include the "18th Graduation Day" scheduled for February 22, 2025, and the "Smart India Hackathon 2025" on September 11, 2025. Other notable programs include workshops on "Design Thinking" and "Photography & Video Editing," focusing on skill development and interdisciplinary learning.
