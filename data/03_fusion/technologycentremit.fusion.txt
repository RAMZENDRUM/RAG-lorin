[SECTION: Overview of Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is approved by AICTE New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University Chennai. The institution focuses on providing quality technical education and skill-based training to meet industrial needs, enhanced by state-of-the-art facilities. The RAISE Centre of Excellence, established in June 2022, aims to transform students into innovators and entrepreneurs, integrating research with education.

[SECTION: RAISE Centre of Excellence]
The RAISE Centre of Excellence (CoE) at MSAJCE, powered by MIT Square, London, focuses on Internet of Things (IoT) and aims to position the institution as an innovation hub. The acronym "RAISE" emphasizes Research, Arts, Innovation, Science, and Entrepreneurship, equipping students and educators with essential skills for technological advancements and business ecosystems in modern sectors.

[SECTION: Collaboration with MIT Square]
MIT Square™ is an ISO-certified company based in Bangalore, recognized by the Ministry of MSME, Govt. of India. It supports MSAJCE through incubation, talent acquisition, and innovation in EduTech, HealthTech, AgroTech, and GreenTech. The collaboration facilitates resources for mentoring, training, and placement of students to cultivate a robust startup community and entrepreneurial ecosystem.

[SECTION: Key Initiatives of RAISE CoE]
The RAISE CoE emphasizes innovative applications for national needs, including Smart City, Smart Health, and Smart Agriculture. Key initiatives focus on developing industry-capable talent and a startup community, while offering online and offline training that ensures hands-on experiences with cutting-edge technology tools.

[SECTION: Benefits of Techno-Entrepreneurship Innovation Lab]
The Techno-Entrepreneurship Innovation lab at MSAJCE provides hardware and software tools for research and development projects. It aims to enhance industry readiness among students and facilitate scalable training programs for corporates and professionals. This lab is recognized as one of the top National Academies for innovation, with a promise of continued support from MIT Square for up to ten years.

[SECTION: Internship and Study Opportunities]
MSAJCE offers extensive internship opportunities, including placements with prestigious companies and study programs abroad. Students can gain personalized LinkedIn recommendations and letters of recommendation upon successful completion of internships, enhancing their career prospects within the globally integrated educational framework.
