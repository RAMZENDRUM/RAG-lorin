[SECTION: Overview of B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science]
The B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is designed to equip students with a robust understanding of artificial intelligence (AI) and data science principles. This four-year program provides core technologies such as data mining, machine learning, and big data analytics. Students develop cross-disciplinary skills, making them proficient in statistics, computer science, and logical reasoning, preparing them for dynamic careers in industries like healthcare, e-commerce, and biotechnology.

[SECTION: Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)]
The program aims to prepare graduates for the complexities of the AI and data science fields. PEO1 focuses on fostering creativity and sustainability in software solutions, while PEO2 emphasizes the importance of modern problem-solving tools for industrial applications. Additionally, PEO3 equips graduates with managerial and leadership skills, paving the way for entrepreneurial ventures in tech.

[SECTION: Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)]
Graduates of the B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science will develop innovative solutions utilizing emerging technologies for real-time applications in industry (PSO1). They will also be capable of applying their acquired knowledge to create sophisticated software solutions and mobile applications for automation (PSO2). These outcomes ensure that students are well-prepared for the demands of the job market.

[SECTION: Career Opportunities and Job Profiles]
The demand for AI and data science professionals in India is projected to grow significantly by 2030. Graduates can pursue diverse roles such as Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, and AI Research Scientist across various sectors including healthcare, finance, and e-commerce. These careers offer lucrative salaries, with entry-level positions starting at ₹6-12 lakhs per annum and experienced professionals earning significantly more.

[SECTION: Faculty Expertise]
The faculty at MSAJCE includes highly qualified individuals with diverse specializations. Notable members include Dr. Nagasubramanian R, Head of PG in Computer Science Engineering, and Dr. Subramanian P, Head of UG focusing on Wireless Sensor Networking. Their extensive knowledge and research in AI and data science provide students with quality education and mentorship.

[SECTION: Admission and Eligibility]
To enroll in the B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, candidates must have completed the 10+2 education system with a pass in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. The program has an intake of 30 students, divided equally between government and management quota seats. This structure ensures a competitive and diverse learning environment.

[SECTION: Vision and Mission]
MSAJCE's vision is to become a center of excellence in AI and Data Science education through sustainable practices. The mission includes imparting fundamental knowledge and critical thinking skills necessary for industry relevance, alongside fostering an environment of creativity and lifelong learning among students.
