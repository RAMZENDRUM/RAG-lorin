[SECTION: Technology Centre at MSAJCE]
The Technology Centre at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering offers a unique hands-on learning experience in 2D drafting and 3D modeling software, enabling students to comprehend and design innovative materials and solutions for modern manufacturing problems. Supported by Altair and CATIA, students gain practical skills such as developing CAD models, executing dynamic and linear static analysis, and preparing assembly drawings. This training ensures graduates are well-equipped for careers in engineering and technology industries.

[SECTION: Networking Academy Partnership]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering has partnered with CISCO to establish the Networking Academy, providing students with essential IT skills through a globally recognized program. The Academy prepares learners for a variety of networking, security, and cloud technology careers, offering courses like CCNA and professional certifications such as the CISCO Certified Network Associate. This collaboration opens pathways to approximately eight million networking job opportunities worldwide.

[SECTION: Courses Offered at MSAJCE]
The courses offered at MSAJCE include a diverse selection, such as Network Essentials, Mobility Fundamentals, and various CCNA programs, including Switching, Routing, and Wireless Essentials. Additionally, students can pursue specialized courses in Security, IoT & Data Analytics, OS and IT, Programming, and Entrepreneurship. This comprehensive curriculum is tailored to enhance employability and prepare students for the evolving demands of the technology sector.
