[SECTION: Responsibilities of the Internal Complaint Committee]
The Internal Complaint Committee plays a pivotal role in maintaining a respectful and safe atmosphere at MSAJCE. It handles grievances and provides legal guidance, ensuring compliance with college policies and regulations. This committee not only addresses complaints but also fosters a culture of awareness regarding rights and responsibilities among students and staff.
