[SECTION: Sports Activities at MSAJCE]
MSAJCE places a significant emphasis on sports activities, aiming to enhance students' physical strength, stamina, and overall well-being. The college has established comprehensive sports facilities that include basketball, football, cricket, volleyball, and a well-equipped fitness center. Engaging in sports fosters improved concentration, stimulates creativity, and develops problem-solving skills, making it integral to personal and academic success. Key events include the Annual Sports Day and participation in zonal and state-level tournaments.

[SECTION: Physical Education Department]
The Physical Education Department at MSAJCE is led by Dr. K. P. Santhosh Nathan, who holds multiple qualifications, including a Ph.D. His role focuses on identifying and developing talent in sports, encouraging participation at collegiate levels, and fostering a culture of physical fitness. The department also provides scholarships for outstanding athletes competing at district, state, and national levels, demonstrating the college's commitment to promoting sportsmanship and skill development.

[SECTION: Sports Facilities and Equipment]
MSAJCE boasts advanced sports facilities designed to support physical education and fitness. The well-equipped gymnasium includes various equipment such as Multi Gyms, Leg Extensions, and Spin Bikes. These facilities cater to the fitness needs of both students and faculty and aim to encourage a healthy lifestyle while promoting competitive spirit. Key pieces of equipment include the Olympic weightlifting bar and triceps ropes, ensuring comprehensive training opportunities for all enthusiasts.

[SECTION: Annual Sports Events]
Annual sporting events at MSAJCE include the Mohamed Sathak Trophy for football and the BSM Trophy for cricket, showcasing the institution's commitment to competitive athletics. These events not only enhance students' sportsmanship but also promote teamwork and community spirit. Participation in inter-university tournaments and local competitions further enables MSAJCE students to gain invaluable experience and recognition in the world of sports.
