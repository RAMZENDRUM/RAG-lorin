[SECTION: Amazon Web Services Education Initiative]
Mohamed Sathak A.J. College of Engineering offers a comprehensive curriculum focused on Amazon Web Services (AWS) and cloud computing through its dedicated Technology Centre. Students will understand the fundamentals of building IT infrastructure on AWS, acquire knowledge of various cloud services, and learn to create and configure AWS services. This training aims to equip students with skills to build fault tolerant and highly available applications, while also embracing best practices to design optimal IT solutions.

[SECTION: CISCO Networking Academy Partnership]
The college has partnered with CISCO to establish the Networking Academy, known for its hands-on learning experience in networking, security, and cloud technologies. This professional certification program, recognized globally, opens up career pathways connecting students to an estimated eight million networking jobs worldwide. Courses include Network Essentials, CCNA certifications, and various security and programming courses, effectively preparing students for the demanding IT industry.
