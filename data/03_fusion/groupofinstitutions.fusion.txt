[SECTION: MSAJCE Overview]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is a premier engineering institution located at 34, Rajiv Gandhi Salai (OMR), Siruseri IT Park, Chennai. Established with a commitment to providing quality education, MSAJCE offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, including B.E., B.Tech, and M.E. The college emphasizes a strong academic environment, supported by a dedicated faculty and modern infrastructure, to equip students with the skills needed for today's job market.

[SECTION: Institutional Structure and Governance]
MSAJCE operates under a well-defined governance structure that includes various committees such as the Governing Council, Planning & Monitoring Board, and the Academic Advisory Committee. These bodies are responsible for maintaining academic standards and implementing educational policies, ensuring that the institution adheres to guidelines set by regulatory agencies like AICTE and NAAC.

[SECTION: Events and Celebrations]
The college hosts significant events, including the Graduation Day, which on February 22, 2025, will celebrate the achievements of its graduating students. Additionally, MSAJCE actively commemorates Teachers and Engineers Day, reflecting its commitment to honoring contributors to education and engineering sectors. Such events foster community spirit and bolster student engagement in extracurricular activities.

[SECTION: Resources and Facilities]
MSAJCE provides a variety of resources designed to support student learning and success. The Learning Centre (Library) offers access to a vast array of academic materials, while additional facilities such as hostels, sports complexes, and campus radio enhance the overall student experience. The college also emphasizes social services and active participation in professional societies, promoting a well-rounded education.

[SECTION: AICTE and Institutional Approval]
MSAJCE is recognized and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated with Anna University. This accreditation underscores the college's dedication to maintaining high educational standards and delivering quality technical education in engineering, preparing students for successful careers in their respective fields.
