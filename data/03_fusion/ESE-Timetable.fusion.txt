[SECTION: MSAJCE Overview]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is a premier engineering institution located in Chennai, India. The college offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.E., B.Tech, and M.E. MSAJCE is committed to providing quality education, supported by a dedicated faculty and modern infrastructure. With a focus on research and innovation, it aims to empower students through a robust curriculum and numerous extracurricular activities.

[SECTION: Available Departments]
MSAJCE offers diverse engineering disciplines including Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical and Electronic Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. Additionally, specialized programs such as Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, along with cybersecurity-focused Computer Science streams, are available. This range of departments ensures a comprehensive education tailored to the evolving tech landscape.

[SECTION: Graduation Events]
The 18th Graduation Day at MSAJCE is scheduled for 22 February 2025, celebrating the achievements of its graduates. Notable events include the “Teachers & Engineers Day 2025” which aims to honor contributions in the field of engineering education. These events foster community building and celebrate academic accomplishments within the college.

[SECTION: Campus Facilities]
The MSAJCE campus features a comprehensive Learning Centre, hostel facilities, and various sports amenities. It promotes an inclusive environment for students, facilitating their overall development through clubs and professional societies. Additionally, there are resources for social services, enhancing the student experience beyond academics.
