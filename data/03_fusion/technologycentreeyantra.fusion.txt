[SECTION: Technology Centre Objectives]
The Technology Centre at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of robotics and embedded systems. The center focuses on teaching the functions of robotic components, robot kinematics, and programming, while also emphasizing safety and economics. Students are equipped to participate confidently in national and international robotics competitions, enabling them to apply robotics solutions to real-life problems. Key activities include hands-on experience with various types of end effectors and sensors.

[SECTION: Networking Academy Partnership]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering has established a partnership with Cisco to create the Cisco Networking Academy. This initiative offers students a unique hands-on learning experience in networking, security, and cloud technologies using simulation tools. The program provides professional certifications recognized globally and career pathways to approximately eight million networking jobs worldwide, highlighting its importance for students aiming for careers in technology.

[SECTION: Courses Offered by the Technology Centre]
The Technology Centre offers a variety of courses designed to build technical expertise in networking and robotics. Courses include Network Essentials, CCNA: Introduction to Networks, and IoT & Data Analytics. Additionally, specialized programs such as CCNA: Switching, Routing, and Wireless Essentials are also part of the curriculum. These courses are structured to guide learners from foundational knowledge to advanced networking skills.

[SECTION: Certification and Career Pathways]
The Technology Centre at MSAJCE certifies students as Cisco Certified Network Associates, equipping them with essential skills for the workforce. Furthermore, students gain insights into implementing Cisco Enterprise Network Core Technologies, opening up numerous career possibilities in the growing field of networking and IT. This dual focus on certification and practical application strengthens the employability of graduates in a competitive job market.
