[SECTION: Robotics Process Automation at MSAJCE]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) has partnered with Automation Anywhere, leaders in Robotic Process Automation (RPA), to establish the AAU powered Bot Lab. This facility offers comprehensive training in RPA, enabling students to automate business processes using software robots. The curriculum includes practical experiences on the Automation Anywhere Enterprise Platform and bot creation methodologies designed to address real-world challenges.

[SECTION: Courses and Certifications Offered]
MSAJCE provides an array of courses in Robotic Process Automation, including certifications for Automation Anywhere Certified Essential RPA Professional, Advanced RPA Professional, and Master RPA Professional. Students receive hands-on training, gain access to industry-recognized certifications, and benefit from internship and placement assistance, enhancing their career prospects in automation technology.

[SECTION: Training in Bot Development]
The RPA program details various training modules, including the creation of bots through recorders and editors available on the Automation Anywhere platform. Students learn to develop Bots aimed at automating manual tasks, significantly boosting productivity across sectors, including industrial, educational, and governmental organizations.

[SECTION: Bot Lab Facilities and Resources]
The AAU powered Bot Lab at MSAJCE is equipped with the resources needed for practical training in software bot development. Participants have opportunities to upload their bots to the Automation Anywhere Bot Store, gaining exposure to the global market for digital workers. The learning environment combines technical knowledge with essential soft skills, ensuring comprehensive development.
