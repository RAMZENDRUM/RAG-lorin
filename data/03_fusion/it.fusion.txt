[SECTION: Overview of MSAJCE]
The Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE), located in Chennai, is committed to advancing engineering and technology education. The college offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.E., B.Tech, and M.E. in Information Technology. With a vision to create proficient IT professionals, MSAJCE emphasizes innovative teaching methodologies, critical thinking, and sustainable practices.

[SECTION: Programs Offered]
MSAJCE offers a B.Tech program in Information Technology designed to be completed in four years, with an option for lateral entry that shortens the duration to three years. The program comprises 8 semesters for regular students and 6 for lateral entry students, with an intake capacity of 60 students evenly split between government and management seats.

[SECTION: Admission Criteria]
To be eligible for admission to the B.Tech. program at MSAJCE, applicants must have completed the 10+2 education system, securing a pass in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. The college encourages prospective students to utilize the online application form available on their website.

[SECTION: Career Opportunities for Graduates]
Graduates from MSAJCE's Information Technology program are prepared for a variety of career paths, including system engineers, software professionals, and network engineers. Additionally, job opportunities extend to roles such as project leaders and academicians across both public and private sectors.

[SECTION: Programme Educational Objectives (PEOs)]
The educational objectives for graduates at MSAJCE include preparing them for designing efficient IT systems and software, equipping them with modern tools for critical problem-solving, and instilling managerial and leadership skills to launch their careers and entrepreneurial ventures.

[SECTION: Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)]
Graduates of the Information Technology program will apply their knowledge to offer innovative solutions in networking and cybersecurity, as well as design and implement mobile applications and software solutions across various automation applications.

[SECTION: Faculty Members]
MSAJCE is staffed with highly qualified faculty members like Dr. Kannan S, a Professor with a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering, and several other dedicated educators, including Dr. Weslin D and Mrs. Aysha Sufreena. Each faculty member brings specialized expertise to enhance the educational experience.

[SECTION: Key Skills Required in IT Domains]
Students can explore diverse fields such as Cloud Computing, AI & Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, and Blockchain. Key skills include programming languages like Python and Java, mastery of modern tools, and certifications like AWS Certified Solutions Architect and Certified Ethical Hacker.

[SECTION: Expected Growth Trends in IT Sector]
The Indian IT industry is forecasted to grow at an annual rate of 7-10% in the next five years. There is a notable shift towards specialized roles like Cloud Engineers, AI Specialists, and Cybersecurity Experts, with salaries in these domains expected to rise significantly.

[SECTION: Salary Expectations for IT Roles]
Projected salaries in the IT sector for specialized roles by 2025 include Cloud Architects earning between ₹12 to ₹24 lakhs, AI/ML Engineers between ₹8 to ₹20 lakhs, and Cybersecurity Experts earning ₹7 to ₹15 lakhs annually, highlighting lucrative opportunities for graduates.
