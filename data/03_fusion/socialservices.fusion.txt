[SECTION: National Service Scheme (NSS) at MSAJCE]
The National Service Scheme (NSS) at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) in Chennai was established in 2001 to promote community service among students. With over 100 active members from various disciplines, the NSS focuses on selfless service and personality development through initiatives like blood donation camps, health awareness programs, and environmental protection campaigns. These activities enhance the knowledge and practical skills of students while bridging the gap between educated and uneducated communities, embodying the spirit of service to the weaker sections of society.

[SECTION: Youth Red Cross (YRC) at MSAJCE]
Founded in 2014, the Youth Red Cross (YRC) at MSAJCE is an initiative aimed at fostering humanitarian principles among students. Currently, the club comprises around 100 active members who engage in various social welfare activities, including health awareness programs and first aid training. The YRC emphasizes nurturing a spirit of service and compassion towards the community, aiming to instill dedication and duty among the youth while promoting inclusivity without discrimination.

[SECTION: Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA)]
Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA) is a transformative initiative inspired by Mahatma Gandhi's vision of sustainable rural development. Launched to address the challenges faced by rural communities, the UBA aims to connect higher education institutions like MSAJCE with local villages. The program focuses on employing eco-friendly technologies and local resources to meet basic needs such as health care, education, and sanitation, thus building an inclusive India through collaborative growth.

[SECTION: Recent NSS Activities at MSAJCE]
In 2023, NSS at MSAJCE conducted several impactful activities, including a Tobacco Awareness Rally and Eye Screening Camp on June 26. Other notable events included a Blood Donation Camp and Financial Education Program earlier in the year, targeting health and education across the local communities. These initiatives reinforce the college's commitment to enhancing the quality of life and promoting social responsibility among students.

[SECTION: YRC Activities for Community Welfare]
The YRC at MSAJCE actively promotes health awareness through programs like free medical and dental camps. On August 11, 2022, the club organized a Sexual Harassment Awareness Programme in various local schools, aiming to empower young minds and ensure safety in the community. This ongoing commitment to social issues establishes a nurturing environment for student volunteers while directly benefiting local residents.

[SECTION: Environmental Initiatives by MSAJCE]
MSAJCE prioritizes environmental sustainability through various initiatives, including tree plantation drives and cleanliness campaigns in local areas like Kaaranai and Siruseri. These activities not only raise awareness about ecological responsibility but also involve students in practical efforts to preserve their local environment, fostering a sense of duty towards nature and community well-being.

[SECTION: Special Programs for Rural Empowerment]
MSAJCE regularly conducts specialized awareness programs to address pressing issues in rural communities. Initiatives like the COVID Vaccination Camps in 2021 targeted local residents in Thazhambur and surrounding villages, highlighting the college's role in public health advocacy. By facilitating these efforts, MSAJCE plays a significant part in equipping the rural populace with necessary resources and information.
