[SECTION: Governing Council Members]
The Governing Council of Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering is led by Alhaj. S.M. Yousuf Sahib, the Chairman of the Mohamed Sathak Trust in Chennai. Other notable members include Dr. T.V. Gopal, a professor from Anna University, and Dr. R. Subramani, the Director at IBM Chennai. The council consists of various senior faculty members, including the Principal as the Member Secretary, and heads of departments such as Academics, Research, and Admissions.

[SECTION: Key Committees]
At MSAJCE, several important committees operate under the Governing Council, including the Planning & Monitoring Board and the Grievance and Redressal Committee. These committees are essential for managing the college's organizational structure and ensuring compliance with educational standards. The focus is on upholding academic integrity while promoting a conducive environment for students and staff.
