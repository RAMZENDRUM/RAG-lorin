[SECTION: College Overview]
The Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE), located at 34, Rajiv Gandhi Salai (OMR), Siruseri IT Park, Chennai, offers a variety of engineering programs including B.E, B.Tech, and M.E degrees. The college aims to provide quality education guided by its vision and mission, focusing on academics, research, and student services. The institution is affiliated with Anna University and is recognized by AICTE.

[SECTION: Admissions Information]
Admissions for the academic year 2026-2027 are now open, with an online application form available on the MSAJCE website. Prospective students can also complete their online fee payments digitally. The college encourages students to engage with various activities such as the internship report workshops and graduation ceremonies, with significant events like the 18th Graduation Day scheduled for February 22, 2025.

[SECTION: Key Features and Services]
MSAJCE provides a broad range of student support services including a learning center, hostel facilities, and transportation. The college promotes extracurricular engagement through clubs, societies, and sports. The Grievance Cell, accessible at grievance@msajce-edu.in, ensures student feedback and concerns are addressed efficiently, further enhancing the campus experience.

[SECTION: Academic Programs and Research Initiatives]
MSAJCE has a structured curriculum and offers numerous academic programs across various engineering disciplines. The college is committed to fostering research through its funded initiatives, including the AICTE-ATAL sponsored faculty development programs designed to enhance instructional quality and academic engagement.
