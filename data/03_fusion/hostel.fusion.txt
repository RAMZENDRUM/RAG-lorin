[SECTION: Boys Hostel Facilities]
The Boys Hostel at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering is designed to accommodate 480 male students in a comfortable environment. It features well-furnished rooms with modern amenities, including cots, chairs, lamps, and Wi-Fi. Each room is shared by two students, ensuring a harmonious living space. Facilities such as a hygienic canteen, an entertainment hall with an LCD TV, and a reading room help enhance students' living conditions and support their academic focus.

[SECTION: Girls Hostel Overview]
The Girls Hostel, located 5 KM away from the campus in Sholinganallur, accommodates 210 female students across 71 rooms. Each room accommodates up to three students and is equipped with essential modern amenities including individual cupboards, bath and toilet facilities, and study areas. With a serene atmosphere and nearby library access till 9:00 PM, the hostel fosters a conducive study environment while ensuring safety and comfort for its residents.

[SECTION: Hostel Mess Services]
The hostel mess at MSAJCE serves both vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals under the management's supervision. With designated timings for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, it accommodates the dietary needs of students on working days and holidays. Mess services ensure a spacious dining area with separate sections for boys and girls, promoting a comfortable dining experience for all hostellers while aligning with the scheduled meal times.

[SECTION: Mess Timings Schedule]
- Breakfast is served from 07:00 AM to 08:00 AM during working days, and from 07:30 AM to 09:00 AM on holidays.
- Lunch is available from 01:00 PM to 01:45 PM on working days, with timings extending to 12:30 PM to 02:00 PM during holidays.
- Dinner is offered from 07:00 PM to 08:30 PM during working days, and from 07:00 PM to 09:00 PM on holidays.

[SECTION: Study Hours and Rules]
The hostel promotes an academic atmosphere with defined study hours to optimize learning. Morning study hours are from 05:00 AM to 07:00 AM, and evening from 06:00 PM to 07:00 PM, alongside additional study times on holidays. Strict rules ensure that students maintain cleanliness and refrain from undesirable activities, enhancing overall discipline within the hostel.

[SECTION: Canteen Committee Leadership]
The canteen committee at MSAJCE is led by Dr. K. S. Srinivasan as President, with Dr. S. Vijayakumar as the Chief Organization Officer. The team includes Mr. Arun as the Manager and Dr. K.P. Santhosh Nathan heading Student Affairs. This leadership structure ensures the effective operation of services catering to students' dining and food preferences, enhancing their campus experience.
