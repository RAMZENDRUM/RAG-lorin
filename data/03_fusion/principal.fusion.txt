[SECTION: Institutional Overview]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is located at 34, Rajiv Gandhi Salai, Siruseri IT Park, Chennai. The institute emphasizes delivering excellent all-round education supported by state-of-the-art facilities. It aims at developing multidimensional students prepared to face challenges associated with Industry 4.0. Key institutional features include a diverse curriculum, various committees for governance, and a robust grievance redressal system.

[SECTION: Principal's Message]
Dr. K.S. Srinivasan, Ph.D., the Principal of MSAJCE, expresses commitment to providing a comprehensive educational environment that fosters students' physical, mental, social, and emotional growth. He highlights the importance of nurturing creative thinking and innovative approaches, assuring prospective students that MSAJCE is dedicated to helping them achieve their best potential and bring laurels to the institute.

[SECTION: Academic Programs and Admissions]
MSAJCE offers various engineering programs, including B.E. and B.Tech degrees, with admissions open for the academic year 2026-2027. Prospective students can apply online and find detailed information about the curriculum and regulations. The college also provides structured support, including an internship report format for students.

[SECTION: Student Life and Facilities]
The college promotes a rich campus life with facilities such as a learning center (library), hostels, transportation, sports amenities, and various clubs and societies. MSAJCE also encourages social services, ensuring a well-rounded experience for its students, weaving together academic and extracurricular opportunities.
