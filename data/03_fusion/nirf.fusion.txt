[SECTION: Institutional Overview]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is located at 34, Rajiv Gandhi Salai (OMR), Siruseri IT Park, Siruseri, Chennai - 603103. The institution, recognized by Anna University and AICTE, offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.E, B.Tech, and M.E. MSAJCE emphasizes a comprehensive education with a focus on research and innovation, aiming to prepare students for successful careers in engineering.

[SECTION: NIRF Rankings]
MSAJCE has consistently improved its standings in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) over the years. In 2026, the college is ranked for engineering, overall performance, and in innovation, showcasing its commitment to educational excellence and robust student outcomes. The institution achieved notable rankings in previous years, including 2025, 2024, and 2023, highlighting its continuous growth and quality education.

[SECTION: Admission Information]
Admissions for the academic year 2026-2027 at MSAJCE are currently open. Prospective students can apply online through the official website, with facilities for online fee payment. The college aims to provide an accessible platform for students to begin their higher education journey in engineering.

[SECTION: Celebrations and Events]
MSAJCE holds various events, including the Graduation Day, celebrated on February 22, 2025, which recognizes the achievements of its students. Additionally, the college celebrates Teachers' and Engineers' Day, emphasizing the contributions of educators and professionals in the engineering field.

[SECTION: Student Services and Facilities]
MSAJCE offers a range of student services, including a well-equipped library, hostels, transport facilities, and various clubs and societies for extracurricular engagement. The institution focuses on holistic development, fostering both academic and social skills among its students through active participation in campus life. 

[SECTION: Support and Resources]
The college maintains a Grievance Cell to address student concerns and ensure a supportive educational environment. MSAJCE also provides a feedback facility available on the AICTE portal, allowing students and faculty to share their insights and contributions towards institutional improvements.
