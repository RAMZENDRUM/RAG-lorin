[SECTION: MSAJCE Overview]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is a prominent engineering institution located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The college offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs including B.E, B.Tech, and M.E, emphasizing a robust academic environment. Established with a vision to promote engineering education, MSAJCE integrates innovative teaching methods and state-of-the-art facilities to foster student learning and development.

[SECTION: Functional Committees]
MSAJCE is supported by multiple Functional Committees responsible for enhancing academic and administrative operations. These committees include the Governing Council, Planning & Monitoring Board, and Academic Advisory Committee among others, all aimed at promoting institutional effectiveness and addressing student needs. The college is committed to continuous improvement through these functional bodies, ensuring a holistic educational experience.

[SECTION: Events and Celebrations]
MSAJCE hosts various significant events, including the annual Graduation Day, with the next scheduled for February 22, 2025. This event celebrates student achievements and includes participation from faculty and dignitaries. Other notable events include the Teachers & Engineers Day celebration and the AICTE-ATAL sponsored Faculty Development Program, which aims to provide faculty with advanced teaching skills.
