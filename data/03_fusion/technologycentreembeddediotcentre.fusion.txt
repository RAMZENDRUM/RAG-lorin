[SECTION: Embedded and IoT Centre Overview]
The Embedded and IoT Centre at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering focuses on educating students about Smart Objects and IoT Architectures. Through hands-on training with Arduino and Raspberry Pi, students will learn about various IoT-related protocols, device integration, and cloud data management. This program aims to develop essential IoT infrastructure and provide real-time applications in sectors like industrial automation and healthcare.

[SECTION: Courses Offered in IoT]
The Centre offers comprehensive courses such as "Introduction to IoT," covering architecture, design principles, and security aspects. Key courses include "Elements of IoT," focusing on hardware and software components, and "IoT Application Development," which introduces device data storage and integration. There are also case studies and mini-projects that encourage practical learning in areas like agriculture and home automation.

[SECTION: Hands-on Practical Learning]
Students engage in practical exercises using Arduino and Raspberry Pi, including coding for various sensors and data management techniques. They learn to interface components, write programs for data collection, and implement communication protocols like MQTT and Bluetooth. These hands-on experiences are designed to cement their theoretical knowledge while preparing them for real-world applications.

[SECTION: Cisco Networking Academy Partnership]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College has partnered with Cisco to establish the Networking Academy. This program offers unique hands-on learning experiences in networking, security, and cloud technologies. Students gain access to professional certifications recognized worldwide, enhancing their pathways into an estimated eight million networking jobs globally.

[SECTION: Certification Opportunities]
The Embedded and IoT Centre emphasizes the value of certification, such as the CISCO Certified Network Associate. Students learn essential networking skills through courses like "CCNA: Introduction to Networks" and "CCNA: Switching, Routing, and Wireless Essentials," equipping them with the knowledge needed for successful careers in technology.

[SECTION: Suggested Reading Materials]
To further enrich their learning, students can refer to suggested books like "Internet of Things: A Hands-on Approach" by Vijay Madisetti and "Designing the Internet of Things" by Adrian McEwen. These resources provide foundational knowledge as well as practical applications relevant to the rapidly evolving field of IoT.
