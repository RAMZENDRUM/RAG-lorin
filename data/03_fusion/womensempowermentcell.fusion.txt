[SECTION: Women's Empowerment Cell]
The Women's Empowerment Cell at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) aims to promote the holistic development of female students through various activities and initiatives. The cell is chaired by Mrs. S. Sudha, who serves as the Coordinator, alongside dedicated members including Mrs. Janaki, Ms. Usha, Mrs. Abida, Mrs. Muthu Pandeeswari, Mrs. N. Sathya, and Ms. Gayathri, all of whom are faculty members at MSAJCE, Chennai. This initiative is crucial in creating a supportive environment that empowers women in engineering.

This facilitates engagement with both students and faculty, ensuring their voices are heard.

[SECTION: Activities and Achievements]
The Women's Empowerment Cell organizes events to celebrate milestones such as Teachers and Engineers Day, enhancing awareness and appreciation of women's contributions in these fields. The upcoming 18th Graduation Day, scheduled for February 22, 2025, will be an event where achievements are recognized, emphasizing the importance of female role models in STEM education.

[SECTION: Student Involvement]
Among the students involved in the Women's Empowerment Cell are Ms. Abinaya Bharathi, who is in her fourth year of Computer Science Engineering, and another student, also named Abinaya, from the third year in Electronics and Communication Engineering. Their participation highlights the active role students play in promoting gender equality and empowerment within the college community.
