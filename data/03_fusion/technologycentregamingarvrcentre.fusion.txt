[SECTION: Gaming and AR/VR Centre at MSAJCE]
The Gaming and AR/VR Centre at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering provides students with hands-on learning experiences in designing and developing applications using Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR). Students will be able to create 3D designs and animations, apply industry-standard tools, and develop applications across various fields such as gaming, architecture, and healthcare. This initiative aligns with global technological trends and enhances students' job readiness.

[SECTION: Curriculum and Courses Offered]
The curriculum for the Gaming and AR/VR Centre includes diverse subjects such as Introduction to Virtual Reality, Interactive Techniques, and Augmented and Mixed Reality. Each module spans approximately six hours and covers both theoretical concepts and practical applications. This structured approach empowers students with the knowledge to navigate the AR/VR landscape and engage in topics like VR hardware, visualization techniques, and physical simulations.

[SECTION: Networking Academy Partnership with CISCO]
MSAJCE has partnered with CISCO to offer the Networking Academy Program, which provides essential IT skills and certifications recognized worldwide. This initiative equips students with practical experience in networking, cybersecurity, and cloud technologies, fostering career pathways to the estimated eight million networking jobs globally. Courses include CCNA certifications and specialized training in IoT and data analytics, ensuring comprehensive professional development.

[SECTION: Practical Applications in VR]
The practical components of the VR curriculum offer projects such as developing virtual classrooms, exploring human anatomy, and simulating physics concepts. These hands-on experiences are designed to deepen students' understanding and application of VR technology in real-world scenarios. For instance, students will participate in experiments using CRO-based simulations and build electronic circuits within a virtual environment.

[SECTION: Certification Opportunities]
Students at MSAJCE have the opportunity to earn certifications such as the CISCO Certified Network Associate, which enhances their employability in competitive job markets. The emphasis on hands-on training through simulation tools ensures that graduates are well-prepared for the technological demands of the industry, further supporting their career advancement in fields related to cyber technologies and networking.
