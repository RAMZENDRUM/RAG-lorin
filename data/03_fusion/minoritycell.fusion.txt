[SECTION: Minority Cell at MSAJCE]
The Minority Cell at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) aims to promote educational and social development among minority communities. Led by Convener Dr. E. Dhiravidachelvi, the cell includes members such as Mrs. Adhilakshmi Logamurthy, an Advocate & Legal Consultant, and Dr. I. Manju from the EEE department. It fosters an inclusive environment by providing support and resources to students from diverse backgrounds.

[SECTION: MSAJCE Governance and Structure]
The governance of MSAJCE is overseen by a comprehensive structure including the Governing Council and various committees such as the Academic Advisory Committee and the Anti-Ragging Committee. This organized framework ensures the institution adheres to high educational standards and policies, fostering a conducive learning environment. 

[SECTION: Core Values and Mission of MSAJCE]
MSAJCE's mission is centered on fostering knowledge, innovation, and character building among its students. The college emphasizes holistic education by integrating technological advancements with traditional learning to prepare students for future challenges in engineering and technology. The institution's vision aligns with creating ethical engineers who contribute positively to society.

[SECTION: Alumni Engagement at MSAJCE]
The Alumni network of MSAJCE plays a pivotal role in maintaining connections and fostering community development. With active participation in events and initiatives, alumni contribute to the college’s vision by mentoring current students and supporting various college programs, thus bridging the gap between education and real-world application.
