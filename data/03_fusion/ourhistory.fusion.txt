[SECTION: MSAJCE Overview]
Mohamed Sathak A.J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is located in Chennai and is renowned for its commitment to providing high-quality education. Established by the Mohamed Sathak Trust in 1984, MSAJCE aims to deliver academic excellence in various disciplines, including Engineering Technology and Science. The institution is recognized as the first self-financing engineering college in India, dedicated to catering to both industry and educational demands. 

[SECTION: Mohamed Sathak Trust]
The Mohamed Sathak Trust was founded on October 26, 1973, in Kilakarai by the philanthropic Mohamed Sathak Family. Its primary objective is to support underprivileged individuals from rural backgrounds in accessing quality education. Over the years, the Trust has established 18 educational institutions across various fields, including Engineering, Nursing, and Medical Sciences, significantly contributing to the region's educational landscape.
