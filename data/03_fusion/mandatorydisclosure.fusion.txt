[SECTION: Overview of MSAJCE]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is a premier engineering institution located in Siruseri, Chennai. Established with a commitment to academic excellence, the college offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.E, B.Tech, and M.E. MSAJCE follows a mission to provide quality education and foster innovation.

[SECTION: Admission Information]
MSAJCE is currently accepting applications for the 2026-2027 academic year. Prospective students can complete the Online Application Form and make Online Fee Payments through the college's official website. The admissions are designed to ensure a streamlined process for applicants.

[SECTION: Graduation Day Events]
MSAJCE celebrates its Graduation Day, with the next event scheduled for February 22, 2025. This annual ceremony recognizes the achievements of graduating students, showcasing their hard work and dedication throughout their academic journey.

[SECTION: Institutional Committees]
The governance at MSAJCE involves several statutory committees including the Governing Council, Planning & Monitoring Board, and Academic Advisory Committee. These bodies ensure compliance with educational standards and promote a supportive academic environment for students.

[SECTION: Student Facilities and Activities]
MSAJCE offers a vibrant campus life with a Learning Centre (Library), Hostel facilities, and various sports activities. The college also features student-run Clubs & Societies that promote extracurricular engagement and professional development among students.
