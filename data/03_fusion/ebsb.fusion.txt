[SECTION: Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat Initiative]
Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat (EBSB) is an initiative launched by the Government of India to enhance unity in diversity. Introduced by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on 31st October 2015, it commemorates Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel's birth anniversary. The program promotes cultural exchange and mutual understanding between various states of India, pairing Tamil Nadu with Jammu & Kashmir. Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering has hosted two programs in collaboration with SSM College of Engineering, fostering cultural ties and educational collaboration.

[SECTION: Cultural Exchange Programs at MSAJCE]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering has conducted cultural exchange programs as part of the Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat initiative. One significant program is the Cultural Exchange Program under EBSB, which aligns with the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav celebrations. Another program focused on the iconic winter destination of Gulmarg, Kashmir, highlighting traditional art forms such as carpet weaving. These initiatives foster cultural understanding and promote best practices across India's diverse regions.
