1. CSI — Computer Society of India
Vision

About

3. IETE — Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers
Established: 16/08/2017 | Active Members: 100
Branch Counselors: Dr. I. Manju, Mrs. S. Anusuya
Goals: Standardized education, practical experience to bridge the "theory-gap."
- Improving the standard of education
- Train students in emerging areas to meet new opportunities
- Encouraging outside-the-classroom studies / practical work / seminars
- Better employment of students on completion of courses
- Substantial increase of IETE membership and nationwide distribution

Benefits of ISF (IETE Students Forum)
- Priority for publishing papers in Journal of Education
- Can subscribe to IETE Journal of Research and IETE Technical Review (available online)
- ISF students can attend IETE technical programs in India at reduced registration fees
- ISF members are issued an Identity Card
