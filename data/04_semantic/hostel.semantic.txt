SECTION: Hostel Facilities

Boys Hostel, which includes the campus, consists of 3 blocks with 233 non-AC rooms and 6 AC rooms, accommodating 2 persons per room. The facility offers essentials such as a cot, chair, lamp, fan, and water heater, along with Wi-Fi and a TV hall. It serves as a home away from home for 480 boy students.

Girls Hostel at Sholinganallur is located 5 KM away from the campus and comprises 1 block with 71 rooms that can accommodate 3 girl students per room. Each room is equipped with modern amenities including a cot, mattress, individual cupboards, and furniture for study. The hostel emphasizes safety and comfort, providing bath and toilet facilities, as well as Wi-Fi access and TV hall.

SECTION: Mess Timings 

For the hostel mess, breakfast is served on working days from 07:00 A.M. to 08:00 A.M., while on holidays, it is from 07:30 A.M. to 09:00 A.M. Lunch is provided from 01:00 P.M. to 01:45 P.M. on working days and from 12:30 P.M. to 02:00 P.M. on holidays. Dinner is available from 07:00 P.M. to 08:30 P.M. on working days and from 07:00 P.M. to 09:00 P.M. on holidays. 

SECTION: Study Hours 

On working days, students have study hours scheduled in the evening from 04:30 P.M. to 06:00 P.M., and in the morning from 05:00 A.M. to 07:00 A.M. On holidays, there are study hours from 09:00 A.M. to 12:00 P.M. and from 01:00 P.M. to 06:00 P.M. The evening study hours on holidays are from 09:00 P.M. to 10:30 P.M.

SECTION: Canteen Committee

SECTION: Rules for Hostellers 

Students must occupy rooms assigned by the Warden (Principal) and must avoid undesirable activities such as alcohol consumption, tobacco use, gambling, and ragging. Students are responsible for maintaining room cleanliness. Parents should provide a list of authorized visitors, and no student is permitted to leave the hostel based solely on a phone call. Visitors are allowed on holidays from 11:00 A.M. to 6:00 P.M.
