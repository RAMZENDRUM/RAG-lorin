SECTION: Overview
The Department of Physical Education at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) places significant emphasis on sports activities as they contribute to enhancing students' physical strength, stamina, and overall well-being. MSAJCE has established essential facilities and programs that promote physical education, understanding that sports are integral to personal development. Research highlights that participation in sports leads to improved blood circulation to the brain, enhancing concentration, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

SECTION: Objectives
The primary objectives of the Physical Education Department include providing opportunities for talent identification, developing identified talents to excel at collegiate and university levels, and fostering a culture of physical fitness, competitiveness, and brotherhood throughout the MSAJCE.

SECTION: Sports Quota Scholarship
MSAJCE encourages sports enthusiasts by offering admissions through a sports quota and scholarships for participants at the district, state, and national levels. The college is equipped with excellent infrastructure to support various sports skills development.

SECTION: Sports Facilities
The campus features various sports facilities, including Basketball, Football, Cricket along with cricket nets, Yoga, Kabaddi, Volleyball, Table Tennis, Carrom, Chess, Track & Field, and Kho Kho, providing comprehensive opportunities for student athletes.

SECTION: Fitness Center
There is a well-equipped gymnasium available for students and faculty at MSAJCE, featuring state-of-the-art equipment laid out in an indoor gym. This facility encourages weight lifters, bodybuilders, and fitness seekers.

SECTION: List of Gym Equipment
The fitness center includes the following equipment: Multi Gym, Leg Extension, Preacher Curl Bench, Multi Adjustable Bench Press, Cable Cross Over Machine, Sitting and Standing Twister, Leg Press, Spin Bike, Round Dumbbells (Rubberised), Rubber Weight Plates, Olympic Curl Bar, Olympic Weight Lifting Bar, Chrome Push-Up Bar, and Triceps Rope.

SECTION: Annual Sports Activities
The department actively engages in organizing and participating in various events, including participation in Anna University, South Zone, and invitation tournaments; conducting Anna University Zonal and Inter-Zonal tournaments; holding an Annual Sports Day; organizing the Mohamed Sathak Trophy for Football and the BSM Trophy for Cricket; and conducting the Fit India Cyclothon.
