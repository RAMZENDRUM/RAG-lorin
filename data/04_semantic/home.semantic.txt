SECTION: Institutional Overview  
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering offers various engineering programs like B.E, B.Tech, and M.E. The institution emphasizes a vision of delivering professional, innovative, and future-ready education to empower students. 

SECTION: Admissions  
Admission is now open for the 2026-2027 academic year. Prospective students can fill out the Online Application Form available on the college website. 

SECTION: Academic Calendar  
The Examination Registration for May-June 2025 is scheduled for the first and second-year as well as third and fourth-year students. The ESE Timetable for November-December 2025 has also been released.

SECTION: Graduation Events  
The 18th Graduation Day is set for 22 February 2025, with celebratory photos available for viewing. 

SECTION: Significant Events  
On 11 September 2025, the Smart India Hackathon 2025 (Internal Edition) is set to take place. A Social Media Awareness Program will be held on 02 September 2025, followed by a Life Skill Empowerment Program on 01 September 2025.
