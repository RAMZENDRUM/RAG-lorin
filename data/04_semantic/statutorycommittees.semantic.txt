SECTION: OVERVIEW
The MSAJCE website provides essential information regarding admissions, academic programs, and the college’s various committees. The website features an Online Application Form and an Online Fee Payment system for prospective students, alongside important announcements such as the celebration of Teachers & Engineers Day 2025, the ESE Timetable for June 2025, and the format for Internship Reports in 2025.

SECTION: GRADUATION DAYS
The 18th Graduation Day is scheduled for February 22, 2025, with photos available afterward. Additionally, there are updates regarding the 17th Graduation Day that also include photos. Admission for the academic year 2026-2027 is currently open, and interested candidates can access the Online Application Form.

SECTION: STATUTORY COMMITTEES
The college has established several statutory committees to ensure a comprehensive educational environment. These include the Governing Council, Planning & Monitoring Board, Academic Advisory Committee, Grievance and Redressal Committee, Anti-Ragging Committee & Squad, Women’s Empowerment Cell, and Functional Committees. Additional committees include the OBC Cell, Minority Cell, SC/ST Cell, and Internal Complaint Committee, which aim to address specific community needs and concerns.

SECTION: ACADEMIC REGULATIONS
The academic framework of the college is structured with defined Regulations, Curriculum, and Syllabus that guide the learning process. The college also emphasizes active participation in Research through technology-driven resources.

SECTION: STUDENT SERVICES
Student facilities at MSAJCE include access to a Learning Centre (Library), Hostel accommodations, Transport services, Sports activities, a Campus Radio station, Social Services initiatives, as well as various Clubs and Societies for extracurricular engagement.

SECTION: ALUMNI AND SCHOLARSHIP
MSAJCE maintains a robust Alumni network and offers AICTE Scholarship options to support students financially. The college emphasizes Incubation initiatives to foster entrepreneurship among its students.

SECTION: ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS
The website acknowledges affiliations with Anna University and approval from AICTE. It also provides access to various policies including the Code of Conduct, Best Practices, and Mandatory Disclosure for transparency in operations. The annual report for the academic year 2023-2024 is also made available.
