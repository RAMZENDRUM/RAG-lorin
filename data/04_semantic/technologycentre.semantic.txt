SECTION: Technology Centres Overview  
In pursuit of the vision and mission of MSAJCE to prepare students for emerging industrial demands, various Technology Centres were established in the year 2019-2020 to educate, train, and upskill students in niche technologies. The courses and training programs are based on the recommendations of AICTE and the India Skill Report 2020. The Technology Centres feature state-of-the-art facilities that provide a conducive environment for research and innovation. Each centre collaborates with industry and government organizations to offer certifications, onsite/offsite projects, and internships. Collaborating industries include Automation Anywhere, Godrej Inc, Ford, Palo Alto Systems, Amazon Web Services, National Instruments, CISCO Academy, Altair, Open Wave Computing, Levergent Technologies, and Propeller Technologies.

SECTION: Centre for 3D Printing and Additive Manufacturing  
The Centre for 3D Printing and Additive Manufacturing provides educational resources and practical training in advanced manufacturing technologies. 

SECTION: Centre for CAE  
The Centre for CAE focuses on computer-aided engineering, offering programs designed to enhance student understanding of engineering simulations and modeling techniques.

SECTION: Centre for Industrial Robotics  
The Centre for Industrial Robotics equips students with practical skills in robotic technologies applied in industrial settings. 

SECTION: Centre for NDT - Non Destructive Testing  
The Centre for NDT - Non Destructive Testing provides training in technologies that evaluate the properties of a material, component, or system without causing damage.

SECTION: Centre for Building Information Modelling  
The Centre for Building Information Modelling offers courses that instruct students on digital representations of physical and functional characteristics of places.

SECTION: Centre for Embedded & IOT  
The Centre for Embedded & IOT specializes in curricula that delve into embedded systems and Internet of Things technologies, preparing students for innovative tech careers.

SECTION: Centre for UAV / Drone Technology  
The Centre for UAV / Drone Technology provides instruction and practical experience in unmanned aerial vehicle technologies.

SECTION: Centre for Godrej Disha Skill Development Centre  
The Centre for Godrej Disha Skill Development Centre partners with industry leaders to enhance skill sets that meet current market needs.

SECTION: Centre for Bosch Industry Institute Collaboration  
The Centre for Bosch Industry Institute Collaboration focuses on collaborative programs that enhance technical skills in partnership with Bosch.

SECTION: Centre for E-Mobility  
The Centre for E-Mobility offers courses on electric mobility solutions, focusing on sustainable transportation technologies.

SECTION: Centre for Renewable Energy  
The Centre for Renewable Energy provides training in alternative energy sources, emphasizing sustainability and environmental responsibility.

SECTION: Centre for APJ Abdul Kalam Innovation Centre  
The Centre for APJ Abdul Kalam Innovation Centre focuses on fostering innovation and entrepreneurship among engineering students.

SECTION: Centre for Bot Lab & Robotics Process Automation  
The Centre for Bot Lab & Robotics Process Automation offers hands-on training in automation technologies and robotics.

SECTION: Centre for Cisco Networking Academy  
The Centre for Cisco Networking Academy provides students with the knowledge and skills needed for networking careers through Cisco's training programs.

SECTION: Centre for Code Tantra - Coding Learning Centre  
The Centre for Code Tantra - Coding Learning Centre offers coding education, equipping students with necessary programming skills.

SECTION: Centre for GAMING AND AR/VR  
The Centre for GAMING AND AR/VR provides training in game design and augmented/virtual reality development.

SECTION: Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Centre  
The Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Centre focuses on equipping students with skills in AI and machine learning technologies.

SECTION: Centre for Block Chain Technology  
The Centre for Block Chain Technology educates students on decentralized technologies and their applications in various industries.

SECTION: Centre for Mobile and Web Application Development  
The Centre for Mobile and Web Application Development provides practical training in developing applications for mobile and web platforms.
