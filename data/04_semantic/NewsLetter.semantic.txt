SECTION: Website Overview
The MSAJCE website, hosted at https://www.msajce-edu.in/NewsLetter.php, serves as an informational platform for Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering, highlighting its offerings in B.E, B.Tech, and M.E programs. Key features include an Online Application Form and Online Fee Payment options.

SECTION: Educational Services
The college promotes several initiatives: 
Teachers & Engineers Day 2025 Celebration Photos are available, alongside the ESE Timetable for June 2025. The Internship Report Format for 2025 can be downloaded, and an overview of the 18th Graduation Day, scheduled for 22 February 2025, is shared with accompanying photos. 

SECTION: Admission Information
The college has opened admissions for the academic year 2026-2027, with details accessible through an Online Application Form and Online Fee Payment option. Promotional materials like the Admission 2026-2027 Flyer are readily available.

SECTION: Faculty Development
A six-day AICTE-ATAL Sponsored Faculty Development Program (FDP) is also being highlighted. 

SECTION: Graduation Highlights
Photos from the 17th Graduation Day are available for viewing, showcasing the achievements and moments from the event. 

SECTION: Contact Information
For inquiries, the Grievance Cell can be contacted via email at grievance@msajce-edu.in. The college also encourages feedback from both students and faculty, accessible through the AICTE Web Portal.

SECTION: Institutional Structure
The college outlines its governance and academic structure, featuring:
- Governing Council
- Planning & Monitoring Board
- Academic Advisory Committee
- Grievance and Redressal Committee 
- Anti-Ragging Committee & Squad 
- Women Empowerment Cell 
- Functional Committees 

SECTION: Academic Programs
The college offers a robust curriculum regulated by specified Academic Regulations and Syllabi across various Departments, alongside a dedicated Technology Centre supporting Research activities.

SECTION: Examination Information
The ESE Timetable for November-December 2025 and Exam Registration details for May-June 2025 for both I & II Year and III & IV Year students are listed, ensuring students have access to necessary academic schedules.

SECTION: Campus Life
Life at MSAJCE encompasses various facilities and services, including a Learning Centre (Library), Hostel amenities, Transport services, Sports activities, Campus Radio, and various Social Services, Clubs & Societies, and Professional Societies, promoting a balanced educational environment.

SECTION: Alumni and Scholarships
The college maintains an active Alumni network and provides information on AICTE Scholarships, as well as opportunities for Incubation and innovation among students.

SECTION: Newsletters
Updates and communications are circulated through Newsletters, with editions NL Vol-1 to NL Vol-5 available for access.

SECTION: Institutional Distinctiveness
MSAJCE is recognized for its adherence to best practices and mandatory disclosures, with approvals from Anna University and AICTE prominently communicated.
