SECTION: PROGRAMME OVERVIEW  
The B.E. Computer Science and Engineering with Specialization in Cyber Security (CSCS) is an undergraduate engineering degree program focused on equipping students with the knowledge and skills needed to protect computer systems, networks, and data from cyber threats and attacks. This course is designed to address the increasing demand for cybersecurity professionals given the reliance on digital infrastructure across sectors. Objective: To develop experts in the field of information security, ethical hacking, and digital forensics.

SECTION: KEY FEATURES  
The program features a specialized curriculum that focuses on security principles, network security, cryptography, ethical hacking, and digital forensics, incorporating foundational courses such as Data Structures, Algorithms, and Operating Systems. It includes hands-on learning labs and simulation environments for penetration testing, secure coding, and threat analysis, along with capstone projects, internships, and real-world problem-solving.

SECTION: VISION AND MISSION  
Vision: To be a center of excellence for transforming students into proficient Cyber Security Specialists through sustainable practices.  
Mission 1: To impart core fundamental knowledge and necessary skills in Cyber Security through innovative teaching and learning methodology.  
Mission 2: To inculcate critical thinking, ethics, lifelong learning, and creativity needed for industry and society.  
Mission 3: To cultivate students with all-round competencies for career, higher education, and self-employability.

SECTION: PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEOs)  
Graduates will be prepared for analysing, designing, developing and testing the software solutions and products with creativity and sustainability. Graduates will be skilled in the use of modern tools for critical problem-solving and analyzing industrial and societal requirements. Graduates will be prepared with managerial and leadership skills for career and starting up their own firms.

SECTION: PROGRAMME SPECIFIC OUTCOMES (PSOs)  
Engineering Graduates will be able to develop creative solutions by adapting emerging technologies/tools for real-time applications of Industry. They will apply the acquired knowledge to develop software solutions and innovative mobile apps for various automation applications.

SECTION: PROGRAMME OUTCOMES (POs)  
Graduates will demonstrate ability in Engineering Knowledge by applying mathematics, science, and engineering fundamentals to solve complex engineering problems. Graduates will identify, formulate, review literature, and analyze complex engineering problems using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences. Graduates will be able to design solutions for complex engineering problems while considering public health, safety, and cultural, societal, and environmental considerations.

SECTION: PLACEMENT OPPORTUNITIES  
With the growing demand for cybersecurity professionals, the Cyber Security sector in India is expected to experience strong growth over the next five years (2025–2030). Key drivers for employment growth include increasing cyber threats, government initiatives like the Personal Data Protection Bill and National Cyber Security Strategy, and the rapid digitization of businesses and digital transformation initiatives.
