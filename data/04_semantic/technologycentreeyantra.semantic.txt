SECTION: Overview
Mohamed Sathak A J College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is located at 34, Rajiv Gandhi Salai (OMR), Siruseri IT Park, Siruseri, Chennai - 603103. For inquiries, contact +91 99400 04500 or email msajce.office@gmail.com. The college offers a range of engineering programs including B.E, B.Tech, and M.E, with admissions open for 2026-2027.

SECTION: Educational Objectives in Robotics
The students will be able to learn the function of the basic components of robots. The students will be able to study the various types of end effectors and sensors. The students will be able to impart knowledge in robot kinematics and programming. The students will be able to learn robot safety issues and economics. 

SECTION: Advanced Robotics Learning
The students will be able to understand the embedded systems and robotics technology. The students will be able to design, develop, program and test the robots for various applications. The students will be able to participate in national and international robotics competitions confidently. The students will be able to use the robots to solve real-life problems.

SECTION: Networking Academy Partnership 
Mohamed Sathak A J College of Engineering has partnered with CISCO, the world leaders in networking, to establish the Networking Academy (NetAcad). Cisco Networking Academy is an IT skills and career-building program which provides a unique hands-on learning experience in the latest networking, security, and cloud technologies using simulation tools. 

SECTION: Certification and Professional Pathway
The program offers a professional certification recognized by employers worldwide and provides career pathways to connect to an estimated eight million networking jobs around the world through NetAcad.

SECTION: Courses Offered through NetAcad
The college offers various courses including Network Essentials, Mobility Fundamentals, CCNA: Introduction to Networks, CCNA: Switching, Routing, and Wireless Essentials, CCNA: Enterprise Networking, Security and Automation or Networking Courses, Security Courses, IoT & Data Analytics Courses, OS and IT Courses, Programming Courses, and Entrepreneurship.

SECTION: Certifications Available
CISCO Certified Network Associate and Implementing CISCO Enterprise Network Core Technologies are among the certifications available through the Networking Academy.
