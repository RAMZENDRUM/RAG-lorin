SECTION: General Information  
The Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is located at 34, Rajiv Gandhi Salai (OMR), Siruseri IT Park, Siruseri, Chennai - 603103. For inquiries, contact them at +91 99400 04500 or msajce.office@gmail.com. 

SECTION: Admissions  
The college is currently accepting online applications for the academic year 2026-2027. Interested candidates can fill out the Online Application Form and make online fee payments.

SECTION: Celebrations  
The 18th Graduation Day is scheduled for 22 February 2025, and photos from the event will be available afterward. The college will also celebrate Teachers & Engineers Day 2025.

SECTION: Courses and Programs  
MSAJCE offers a variety of programs including B.E, B.Tech, and M.E degrees. Detailed academic structures including curriculum and syllabi are provided by the Academic Advisory Committee.

SECTION: Committees  
The college consists of various committees such as the Governing Council, Planning & Monitoring Board, Grievance and Redressal Committee, and Women's Empowerment Cell. Each of these bodies helps in maintaining the standards and welfare within the institution.

SECTION: Facilities  
The campus provides various facilities such as a Learning Centre (Library), hostels, transport services, sports facilities, campus radio, and numerous clubs and societies.

SECTION: Research Initiatives  
Research opportunities are available through the Technology Centre, fostering innovative projects and collaboration among students and faculty.

SECTION: Alumni and Recognition  
MSAJCE emphasizes building a strong alumni network and is recognized by multiple authority bodies including NAAC, IQAC, NIRF, and ARIIA.

SECTION: Institutional Distinctiveness  
The college is committed to best practices in education and governance, demonstrated through its AICTE-approved courses and robust policies for student welfare such as the Grievance Cell.

SECTION: Contact Information
