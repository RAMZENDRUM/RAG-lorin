SECTION: Overview
The Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering, located at 34, Rajiv Gandhi Salai (OMR), Siruseri IT Park, Siruseri, Chennai - 603103, is dedicated to being an eminent institute for higher education and research through innovative teaching-learning and sustainable practices that meet industrial and societal needs.

SECTION: Vision and Mission
The vision of MSAJCE is "To be an eminent institute for higher education and research through innovative teaching-learning and sustainable practices to meet the industrial and societal needs." The mission of MSAJCE is to impart quality technical education by transforming students into professionally competent technocrats, nurturing students in all-round sustainable development for career and self-employability, and instilling critical thinking, professional ethics, and civic responsibilities through values.

SECTION: Quality Policy
MSAJCE is committed to creating quality professionals via innovative teaching, research, industrial relations, and nurturing human values and ethics. The approach is aim-focused on continual improvement to meet both industrial and societal needs.

SECTION: Academic Structure
The academic unfolding includes structured regulations, curriculum, and syllabus outlined for different engineering departments, along with facilities such as the Learning Centre (Library), Hostel, and Sports amenities available for students to ensure a well-rounded educational experience.

SECTION: Institutional Distinctiveness
MSAJCE's commitment to excellence is further demonstrated through frameworks like NAAC, IQAC, NIRF, and ARIIA which underline its dedication to quality education and research. The college also emphasizes industrial relations and ethical practices as foundational elements of its academic philosophy.
