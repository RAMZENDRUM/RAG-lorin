SECTION: Overview of MSAJCE
The Mohamed Sathak A.J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is affiliated to Anna University Chennai and the Government of Tamil Nadu, and it is approved by AICTE New Delhi. MSAJCE operates under the Mohamed Sathak Trust, striving to continuously enhance its facilities to provide quality technical education meeting industrial and societal needs.

SECTION: RAISE Centre of Excellence
The RAISE Centre of Excellence (CoE), established in June 2022, is powered by MIT Square London. It aims to transform students and academic professionals in aspects including Research, Arts & Applications, Innovation & Intelligence, Science & Technology, and Entrepreneurship.

SECTION: Key Initiatives - Innovation & Research
The MSAJCE promotes Innovation & Research through numerous initiatives. These include transforming Ideas to Prototypes & Products, facilitating Inductive Programs, and enhancing Internships & Talent Transformation. The institution encourages International Publications and hosts I-Centric Events while also focusing on IP Rights & Protection.

SECTION: Technological Development and Entrepreneurship
MSAJCE fosters Intrapreneurship & Entrepreneurship, and it strengthens Institution & Industry Connects. The institution also supports Incubation, Investments & Fundings, which allows students to work on real-world projects.

SECTION: Global Opportunities 
Students at MSAJCE have access to international opportunities such as Abroad Studies, Abroad Traineeships, Abroad Internships, and Abroad Placementships. The institution also provides Abroad Mentorships, Fellowships, and Inventorships, promoting Startup Founderships and exposure to Disruptive Technologies.

SECTION: Objectives of RAISE CoE
The objectives of the RAISE CoE include creating innovative applications for Smart City, Smart Health, Smart Manufacturing, and Smart Agriculture. It aims to build industry-capable talent and an entrepreneurial ecosystem, providing a nurturing environment for research and innovation.

SECTION: Training and Learning
MSAJCE emphasizes providing training and learning (including hands-on internships) to students, faculty, and research scholars using world-class equipment, software, and tools. It focuses on establishing an environment conducive to product creation, testing, and incubation.

SECTION: Skills Development
The institution aims to develop application platforms for skill development based on industry needs, producing a new generation of entrepreneurs ready to leverage start-up facilities. Additionally, it creates a core group of researchers in emerging technologies.

SECTION: Techno-Entrepreneurship Innovation Lab
The Techno-Entrepreneurship Innovation lab at MSAJCE is equipped with hardware and software tools for development and research projects. The lab has received recognition as one of the top National Academies for innovation.

SECTION: Curriculum and Support
MSAJCE provides essential curriculum and resource kits to ensure industry readiness for students, along with support for applying for research funds. It offers scalable infrastructure and up to 10 years of assured training support from MIT Square.

SECTION: Leadership and Opportunities
Students have a great opportunity to gain leadership experience, become coding gurus, and spread Techno-Entrepreneurship culture within the campus through events, workshops, and hackathons. There are internship opportunities that lead to personal endorsements on platforms like LinkedIn.

SECTION: International Exposure
Students can intern or work full-time abroad, enhancing their academic profiles. They receive personalized LinkedIn recommendations and letters of recommendation upon successful completion of internships or technology programs.
