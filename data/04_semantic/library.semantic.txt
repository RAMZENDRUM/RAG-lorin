[SECTION: Library Overview]
The library at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering serves as the heart of the institution, fostering academic growth since its inception. It provides critical resources and services designed to meet the scholarly needs of students and staff. The library spans an area of 8978 square feet and is fully computerized, featuring over 29853 volumes, including 1885 reference books.

[SECTION: Collections and Resources]
The library houses a diverse collection including:
- 29853 total volumes.
- 5628 titles.
- 1885 reference books.
- 35 national journals.
- 20 magazines.
- 3790 e-books.
- 5 daily newspapers.
- 106 back volumes.
- 356 CD-ROMs.
- 260 project reports.
- 1379 e-journals via DELNET.
- 1800 international journals in the Gale database.

[SECTION: Library Working Hours]

[SECTION: Rules & Regulations]
All staff and students are library members. Eligibility details include:
- UG and PG students can borrow 18 books for 30 days.
- Teaching staff can borrow 10 books for 30 days.
- Supporting and administrative staff can borrow 4 books for 30 days.
Reference materials and periodicals are not available for borrowing. Silence must be maintained in the library.
