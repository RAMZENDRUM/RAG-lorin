SECTION: Overview of MSAJCE Technology Centre  
Mohamed Sathak A J College of Engineering has established a Technology Centre focusing on Gaming and Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR). The Centre aims to provide students with a comprehensive education in the technical and design aspects of AR and VR, enabling them to create 3D designs, visualizations, and animations for various objects and scenes. Additionally, students will apply AR and VR methods, tools, and equipment across different industries, educational sectors, and social contexts.

SECTION: Learning Objectives  
Upon completion of the program, the students will be able to:
- Learn about the technical and design aspects of AR and VR.  
- Create 3D design, visualization, and animation for various objects and scenes.  
- Apply virtual and augmented reality methods, tools, and equipment in various fields like industry, education, and social life.  
- Provide an overview of the concepts and working of AR-VR techniques.  
- Build basic applications considering the technical and design aspects of AR and VR.  
- Develop PC and mobile-based VR applications.  
- Develop 3D visualization projects.  
- Design applications in fields such as gaming, architecture, manufacturing, retail, and healthcare.

SECTION: Networking Academy Partnership  
MSAJCE has partnered with CISCO to establish the Networking Academy (NetAcad). This collaboration focuses on providing a unique hands-on learning experience in the latest networking, security, and cloud technologies using simulation tools. The program also aims at connecting students with an estimated eight million networking jobs around the world through NetAcad.

SECTION: Professional Certifications  
Students can gain the following professional certification recognized by employers worldwide:
- CISCO Certified Network Associate  
- Implementing CISCO Enterprise Network Core Technologies  

SECTION: Practical Learning Experiences  
Students engage in numerous practical applications, which include:
- Developing architecture for a house using Virtual Reality.  
- Performing CRO-based experiments using Virtual Reality.  
- Undertaking qualitative analysis in Chemistry using Virtual Reality.  
- Carrying out assembly/disassembly of an engine using Virtual Reality.  
- Exploring human anatomy using Virtual Reality.  
- Simulation of various scenarios including circulation of blood in the heart, and flight dynamics of an aircraft.  
- Building electronic circuits using Virtual Reality, utilizing basic electronic components.  
- Developing the concept of a virtual classroom with multiplayer functionalities.
