The college aims to provide innovative teaching-learning and sustainable practices to meet industrial and societal needs. MSAJCE offers a range of programs, including B.E, B.Tech, M.E, and more, with a focus on holistic, multi-disciplinary skill-based education.

[SECTION: National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC)]
The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) is an autonomous institution that assesses and accredits higher education institutions in India. NAAC's vision is to make quality assurance an integral part of the functioning of Higher Education Institutions (HEIs). The council has a general council, executive committee, and advisory committees to steer its policies and activities.
