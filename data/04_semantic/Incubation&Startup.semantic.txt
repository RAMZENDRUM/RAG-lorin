SECTION: Incubation and Startup Overview  
The Sathak Innovation and Incubation Foundation aims to promote commerce, science, education, and research by helping, establishing, and incubating startups, individuals, and teams with innovative ideas to make prototypes and convert them into products. Additionally, the foundation promotes a culture of innovation-driven entrepreneurship in various fields, including science, engineering, technology, commerce, agriculture, health care, and more. It seeks to develop and manage infrastructure for prototype testing and innovation from initial idea stages to full-scale commercial production, collaborating with local and international institutions.

SECTION: Strategic Plans for Innovation  
The institution aims to develop strategies and governance for promoting innovation and entrepreneurship, creating pathways for startups, and building organizational capacity alongside human resources. Collaboration and knowledge exchange are emphasized, alongside providing incubation and pre-incubation support through SIIF.

SECTION: Objectives and KPIs  
The vision includes increasing the self-employment rate with a goal of establishing an environment supportive of innovation and entrepreneurship. Desired outcomes include developing entrepreneurship orientation among students and faculty, formation of five early-stage startups annually, and handling IPR/innovative ideas.

SECTION: Activities and Programs  
The foundation organizes various activities, including workshops on entrepreneurship, design thinking sessions, and field visits to identify real-life problems. Events like Demo Days, business plan contests, and hackathons are conducted annually to foster innovation.

SECTION: Monthly and Annual Evaluation  
Ongoing evaluation involves quarterly newsletters, biannual surveys, and monthly progress reports. The progress reports will track the number of beneficiaries accessing facilities and programs designed to enhance entrepreneurship.
