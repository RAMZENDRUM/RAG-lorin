SECTION: PROGRAM OVERVIEW
The B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS) is a specialized undergraduate engineering program designed to blend core computer science education with insights into business systems and management. It is a relatively new course introduced in collaboration with industry leaders like Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) to bridge the gap between traditional computer science education and the dynamic needs of the IT and business world. This program aims to enhance the relevance of the Computer Science program in India to meet the future demands of the IT industry, whose landscape is rapidly changing in the era of Business 4.0.

SECTION: ACADEMIC OBJECTIVE
The objective of the CSBS program is to produce graduates who are not only technically sound in computer science but also understand the fundamentals of business, economics, and management.

SECTION: KEY FEATURES
The curriculum is designed to ensure that students receive better exposure to emerging subjects such as Analytics, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Business Analytics, Financial Management, Economics, Internet of Things, and Design Thinking. Additionally, the program develops an appreciation towards humanity and value-added science.

SECTION: PROGRAMME DURATION AND DETAILS
The B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems is a 4-year regular program or a 3-year lateral entry program. The number of semesters consists of 8 for regular students and 6 for lateral entry students. The total intake for the program is 30 seats, divided equally between government and management categories.

SECTION: ELIGIBILITY
Candidates must have completed the 10+2 system of education with a pass in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in the qualifying examination.

SECTION: VISION AND MISSION
The vision of the program is to be a center of excellence for transforming students into proficient IT professionals with business skills through sustainable practices. The mission includes imparting core fundamental knowledge and necessary skills in Information Science and Business through innovative teaching methodologies.

SECTION: PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES
Graduates of the program will be prepared for analyzing, designing, developing, and testing software solutions with creativity and sustainability. They will be skilled in using modern tools for critical problem solving and analyze industrial and societal requirements.

SECTION: PROGRAM SPECIFIC OUTCOMES
Graduates will be able to develop creative solutions by adapting emerging technologies for real-time applications in the industry. Additionally, they will apply acquired knowledge to develop software solutions and innovative mobile applications for various automation applications.

SECTION: PROGRAM OUTCOMES
Graduates will apply knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals to solve complex engineering problems. They will identify and analyze complex engineering problems, design solutions that meet specified needs considering public health and safety, and demonstrate knowledge of sustainable professional engineering solutions.
