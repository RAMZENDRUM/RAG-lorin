SECTION: Overview

The MSAJCE provides employment opportunities for students in reputed companies. They organize on and off-campus recruitment drives for students and train them in employability skills such as aptitude, communication, and technical skills. Value-added certification courses are conducted to gain domain-specific knowledge, and mock aptitude tests, mock interviews, and mock group discussions are arranged to prepare students for interviews. The college also facilitates in-plant training, internships, and industrial visits while organizing workshops on recent technologies by industrial experts to benefit students.

SECTION: Facilities

MSAJCE features well-furnished air-conditioned rooms for written tests, group discussions, and face-to-face interviews. There are well-established computer labs for online training and examinations, air-conditioned auditoriums, seminar halls, and meeting halls, as well as dedicated AC rooms for conducting group discussions. Career counseling is available, guiding students to choose the best possible stream to meet their unique requirements, with mentors assisting them in focusing on their targets.

SECTION: Industry Partnerships

The third Industry Institution Interaction Cell aims to bridge the gap between industry and academic institutions. They sign MoUs with industries for mutual benefit. The objectives also include organizing workshops, conferences, and seminars with joint participation from the institute and industries, as well as encouraging faculty to undergo training in various industries to upgrade their knowledge. Engaging industrial experts is emphasized, and industrial visits, internships, and projects are arranged for students.
