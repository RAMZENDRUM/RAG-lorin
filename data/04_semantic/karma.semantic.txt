SECTION: Overview of MSAJCE
The Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is recognized as a premier engineering institution in Chennai, providing various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The college is dedicated to delivering high-quality education and fostering skill development among students.

SECTION: Objectives of KARMA
The "Kaushal Augmentation and Restructuring Mission of AICTE" (KARMA) aims to create a skilled and certified workforce to enhance India's position as a global skills capital. It encourages institutions to utilize available infrastructure for skill training and mandates participation for candidates who have completed at least 10th standard or ITIs.

SECTION: Skill Training Models under KARMA
KARMA encompasses three skilling models designed for different educational contexts:
- Model 1 targets school dropouts after class 10th.
- Model 2 enhances skill advancement for students currently enrolled in AICTE-approved technical institutions, offering advanced courses during the 4th to 7th semesters. 
- Model 3 seeks to integrate school education with higher education, promoting skill-based training for nearby school students once a week.
