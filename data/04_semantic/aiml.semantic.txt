SECTION: Program Overview  
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a technology that enables machines to replicate human behavior, while Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI that allows machines to learn from past data without explicit programming. The B. Tech in Computer Science Engineering with a specialization in AI & ML provides budding engineers with comprehensive courses dedicated to the latest advancements in this field. This program enhances students' skills in programming, coding, database management, and web development, positioning them for future demands in various sectors including healthcare, finance, and security.

SECTION: Admission and Curriculum  
The program duration is 4 years for regular students and 3 years for lateral entry students, with a total intake of 30 students (15 government and 15 management). Eligibility requires a 10+2 education with a pass in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

SECTION: Programme Educational Objectives (PEOs)  
Graduates will be prepared to analyze, design, develop, and test software solutions creatively and sustainably. Graduates will possess skills using modern tools for problem-solving and analyzing industrial and societal needs. Graduates will develop managerial and leadership abilities for careers and entrepreneurship.

SECTION: Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)  
Engineering graduates will be able to develop creative solutions using emerging technologies for real-time industry applications and apply acquired knowledge to create software solutions and innovative mobile apps for various automation needs.

SECTION: Programme Outcomes (POs)  
Graduates will apply mathematics and engineering knowledge to solve complex problems, analyze and review literature for engineering challenges, design solutions considering public safety and cultural contexts, and utilize research methods to reach valid conclusions. They will demonstrate modern tool usage, ethical practices, teamwork, effective communication, project management principles, and engage in lifelong learning.

SECTION: Labs and Facilities  
The institution hosts various specialized labs, including the C Programming Lab for CS8261, Internet Programming Lab for CS8661, Operating Systems Lab for CS8461, Object Oriented Analysis and Design Lab, Web Technology Lab for IT8511, and Networks Lab for CS8581, catering to the needs of current engineering students.

SECTION: Curriculum Syllabus  
Subjects offered include Discrete Mathematics (MA3354), Digital Principles and Computer Organization (CS3351), Database Design and Management (AD3391), Design and Analysis of Algorithms (AD3351), Data Exploration and Visualization (AD3301), and Artificial Intelligence (AL3391). Additional courses in Practical Labs support theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience.

SECTION: Career Prospects  
Graduates will find opportunities as Big Data Engineers, Data Scientists, AI Engineers, and Machine Learning Engineers in reputed companies such as Microsoft, Amazon, Goldman Sachs, and Accenture. The program ensures industry relevance, preparing students for a wide range of exciting career pathways with lucrative salaries, often exceeding that of typical engineering roles. 

SECTION: Research and Development  
India's AI and ML sectors are projected for substantial growth from 2025 to 2030 due to rising adoption across multiple industries, government initiatives supporting AI, and increased investments in startups. The course curriculum will also emphasize hands-on experience with powerful tools like Python, R, TensorFlow, Hadoop, and others essential for the evolving tech landscape.
