SECTION: COLLEGE OVERVIEW  
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering, located at 34, Rajiv Gandhi Salai (OMR), Siruseri IT Park, Siruseri, Chennai - 603103, can be contacted at +91 99400 04500 or msajce.office@gmail.com. The college offers various engineering courses including B.E, B.Tech, and M.E.

SECTION: MANAGEMENT  
Mohamed Sathak Trust, established on 26 October 1973, serves as a charitable and educational organization aimed at providing quality education to deprived communities in rural and financially challenged backgrounds. The Trust pioneered the first self-financing engineering college in India in 1984.

Admissions for the 2026-2027 academic year are currently open, with an online application form available. Interested candidates are encouraged to apply through the MSAJCE website.
