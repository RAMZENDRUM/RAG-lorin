SECTION: Overview of Robotics Process Automation

Mohamed Sathak A J College of Engineering has partnered with Automation Anywhere, the market leaders in Robotic Process Automation (RPA), to establish the AAU powered Bot Lab. This center provides training in RPA, which is a powerful automation software used to create software robots that can automate business processes.

SECTION: Objectives of RPA Training

The students will be able to explain in detail about the Automation Anywhere Enterprise Platform, Architecture, and its Components. The training aims to provide in-depth knowledge about the various features and functionalities of AA software, including the Runtime AA Client and Web Control Room. Students will learn to create Bots using different types of Recorders, Editors, and Basic Commands on the Automation Anywhere platform. They will also apply bot creation methodologies to develop bots that solve real-world problems.

SECTION: Automation Capabilities

The students will automate any sort of manual or repetitive human tasks using the Bot Development tool. They will develop and deploy software bots capable of performing automation tasks 24/7 without interruption. Furthermore, they will create and deploy bots that significantly improve the process and productivity of industrial, educational, government, and private organizations. The training includes building IQ bots with artificial intelligence to handle complex real-world problems.

SECTION: Training Benefits

Participants will gain access to the Automation Anywhere Enterprise Platform and receive hands-on training. They will also obtain globally recognized certifications, with internships and placement assistance across industries. There is an opportunity for students to build their own bots and upload them on the Automation Anywhere Bot Store, the leading marketplace for bots and Digital Workers.

SECTION: Certification Offered

The training program offers multiple certifications: Automation Anywhere Certified Essential RPA Professional, Automation Anywhere Certified Advanced RPA Professional, and Automation Anywhere Certified Master RPA Professional.

SECTION: Course Structure

The course includes several components:
1. Introduction to Robotic Process Automation & Bot Creation for 6 hours.
2. Web Control Room and Client training for 6 hours.
3. Bot Creator instruction lasting 9 hours.
4. Training on Meta Bot and Bot Insight for 6 hours.
5. Assessment and Essential Certification for 6 hours.
6. A list of experiments totaling 12 hours.

SECTION: Practical Applications

Students will experience practical applications such as installing the AA Control Room, SQL Server, and AA Client, as well as bot creation using various recorders and command libraries. They will learn to automate Excel operations, PDF integrations, and develop bots using Object Cloning Command and error handling. Meta Bot Designing with AI Sense will also be covered.

SECTION: Textbooks for Reference

Recommended textbooks include "Learning Robotic Process Automation: Create Software robots and automate business processes with the leading RPA tool - UiPath" and "Robotic Process Automation A Complete Guide - 2020 Edition."
