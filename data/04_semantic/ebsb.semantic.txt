SECTION: EBSB Initiative Details
Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat (EBSB) is an initiative launched by the Government of India to enhance unity in diversity of our nation, India. EBSB was launched by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi during the Rashtriya Ekta Divas held on 31st October, 2015, to commemorate the birth anniversary of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel to establish a sustained and structured cultural connection between denizens of different regions of India. The EBSB programme aims to enhance interaction and promote mutual understanding between people of different states and union territories through the concept of state pairing. Tamil Nadu is paired with Jammu-Kashmir.
