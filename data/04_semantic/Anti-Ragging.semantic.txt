[SECTION: Introduction to Anti-Ragging Policy]
Ragging in any form is totally prohibited in and outside the campus of Mohamed Sathak AJ College of Engineering. The college aims to make its campus a 'Ragging free' Institution, ensuring a conducive environment for freshers to adapt to college life. Dr. K.S. Srinivasan, the Principal of MSAJCE, heads the Anti-Ragging Committee. The committee consists of faculty members, including Dr. S. Devikala, Head of Student Affairs, and Dr. A. Balakrishnan, Head of Administration.

[SECTION: What Constitutes Ragging?]
Ragging constitutes one or more of the following acts: teasing, treating, or handling with rudeness a fresher or any other student, indulging in indiscipline activities that cause annoyance or harm, asking a student to do an act that causes shame or embarrassment, and any act of physical or psychological abuse. Dr. R. Someswaran, Assistant Professor of Civil Engineering, and Dr. S. Prasath, Assistant Professor of Mechanical Engineering, are members of the Anti-Ragging Committee and help identify such acts.

[SECTION: Punishments for Ragging]
Punishments for ragging include suspension from classes, withholding of scholarships, debarring from exams, and expulsion from the hostel or institution. The Anti-Ragging Committee, headed by Dr. K.S. Srinivasan, determines the punishment based on the nature and gravity of the offense. Mr. V. Vigneshwaran, Head of Placement, and Mr. A. Abdul Gafoor, Admission Officer, are also part of the committee and help in the decision-making process.
