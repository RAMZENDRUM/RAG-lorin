SECTION: Overview of Departments
There are various departments at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering, including Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Electronic and Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, Computer Science and Business Systems, Computer Science and Engineering with specialization in Cyber Security, and options for HONOR or MINOR specializations. Additionally, there is a collective designation for First Year students covering all departments.

SECTION: Postgraduate Opportunities
For postgraduate studies at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering, the available departments are Computer Science and Engineering, and Structural Engineering. 

SECTION: Graduation Celebrations
The 18th Graduation Day event is scheduled for February 22, 2025, showcasing the achievements of graduates from the college.

SECTION: Academic Calendar and Admission
The college is currently accepting admissions for the academic year 2026-2027, and prospective students can submit their applications online. 

SECTION: Faculty and Administration Structure
The administrative structure includes several committees such as the Governing Council, Planning & Monitoring Board, Academic Advisory Committee, Grievance and Redressal Committee, Anti-Ragging Committee & Squad, and the Women’s Empowerment Cell. 

SECTION: Campus Facilities 
Life at MSAJCE encompasses access to a Learning Centre (Library), hostel accommodations, transport services, sports facilities, campus radio, and various clubs and societies, enhancing the overall student experience. 

SECTION: Online Facilities
The college offers online services including an application form for admissions, online fee payment, and feedback facilities available on the AICTE web portal.
