[SECTION: Program Overview]
B.Tech in Electronics Engineering with Specialization in VLSI Design and Technology is designed to meet the increasing demand for integrated chips and sensors in the digital economy and consumer electronics. The global semiconductor market, valued at $590 billion in 2021, is anticipated to reach $1 trillion by 2030, driven by growth in technologies like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Electric Vehicles (EV), 5G Networks, and the Internet of Things (IoT).

[SECTION: Curriculum and Specialization]
The VLSI curriculum encompasses a range of topics including Semiconductor device fundamentals, Analog and Digital circuits, VLSI circuit design, IC design technology, semiconductor equipment operation, and Embedded systems. Hands-on experience with Electronic Design Automation (EDA) tools is provided, preparing engineers for the dynamic demands of the semiconductor industry.

[SECTION: Educational Objectives]
Programme Educational Objectives (PEOs) specify that graduates will be prepared for analyzing, designing, developing, and testing software solutions with creativity and sustainability. They will acquire skills in modern tools for problem-solving and will be equipped with managerial and leadership skills for their careers.

[SECTION: Program Specific Outcomes]
Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs) include developing creative solutions using emerging technologies for real-time applications and applying knowledge to create innovative software solutions and mobile apps for various automation applications.

[SECTION: Programme Outcomes]
Programme Outcomes (PO) detail essential competencies such as Engineering Knowledge, Problem Analysis, Design Development, Investigative Skills, Modern Tool Usage, societal awareness, and individual/team effectiveness. The curriculum also emphasizes ethics, communication, project management, and the importance of life-long learning.

[SECTION: Job Market and Growth]
The VLSI Design industry is projected to grow significantly from 2025 to 2030, with key hiring sectors including Semiconductor Design, Telecommunication, Automotive Electronics, IoT, and AI/ML Hardware. The demand for roles such as VLSI Design Engineer, Verification Engineer, and RFIC Design Engineer will rise, driven by advancements in technology.

[SECTION: Faculty Expertise]
The esteemed faculty includes Dr. NAGASUBRAMANIAN R (Head - PG, Computer Science Engineering), Dr. SUBRAMANIAN P (Head - UG, Wireless Sensor Networking and Image Processing), Dr. PREMALATHA G (Associate Professor, Image Processing and Machine Learning), and several other qualified professionals specializing in areas like IoT and Cloud Computing, and Network Security.

[SECTION: Laboratory Facilities]
Laboratories such as the C Programming Lab, Internet Programming Lab, Operating Systems Lab, and Web Technology Lab are available to provide students with hands-on experience. Each lab offers specific courses, such as CS8261 - C Programming and IT8511 - Web Technology, complete with online resources and documentation for student reference.

[SECTION: Internship and Placement]
Internship opportunities and placement services are integral parts of the program, connecting students with industry leaders and ensuring a smooth transition from academic learning to professional work experiences.
