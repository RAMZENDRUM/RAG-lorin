SECTION: Overview of the Embedded & IoT Centre
Mohamed Sathak A J College of Engineering established the Embedded & IoT Centre to provide students with a comprehensive education in Internet of Things (IoT) related technologies. The Centre aims to teach students Smart Objects and IoT Architectures. The curriculum includes various IoT-related protocols and practical applications.

SECTION: Objectives of the Centre
The students will be able to learn the Smart Objects and IoT Architectures. The students will learn about various IOT-related protocols. The Centre encourages students to build simple IoT Systems using Arduino and Raspberry Pi. Students will be trained to understand data analytics and cloud in the context of IoT. Additionally, they will develop the IoT infrastructure for popular applications.

SECTION: Learning Outcomes
The students will be able to understand the concept of IoT. They will analyze various protocols for IoT. The programme includes designing a Proof of Concept (PoC) of an IoT system using Raspberry Pi/Arduino. Students will apply data analytics and utilize cloud offerings related to IoT. They will also analyze applications of IoT in real-time scenarios.

SECTION: Networking Academy Partnership
Mohamed Sathak A J College of Engineering has partnered with CISCO, the world leaders in networking, to establish the Networking Academy (NetAcad). This collaboration provides students with unique hands-on learning experiences in the latest networking, security, and cloud technologies using simulation tools. 

SECTION: Professional Certification and Career Pathways
Students will gain a professional certification recognized by employers worldwide. This certification opens career pathways to connect to an estimated eight million networking jobs globally through NetAcad.

SECTION: Courses Offered
The Centre offers several courses including Network Essentials, Mobility Fundamentals, CCNA: Introduction to Networks, CCNA: Switching, Routing, and Wireless Essentials, and CCNA: Enterprise Networking, Security and Automation OR Networking Courses. Other courses include Security Courses, IoT & Data Analytics Courses, OS and IT Courses, Programming Courses, and Entrepreneurship.

SECTION: Certifications
Students can achieve certifications such as CISCO Certified Network Associate and Implementing CISCO Enterprise Network Core Technologies.

SECTION: IoT Course Details
The IoT programme includes courses like Introduction to IoT (8 Hours), which covers the Architectural Overview, Design principles, IoT Applications, Sensing, Actuation, Basics of Networking, M2M and IoT Technology Fundamentals, Data management, Business processes in IoT, Everything as a Service (XaaS), Role of Cloud in IoT, and Security aspects in IoT. Another key course is Elements of IoT (9 Hours), focusing on Hardware and Software Components using Arduino and Raspberry Pi.

SECTION: Practical Training
The Centre emphasizes practical training through detailed sessions. Students will familiarize themselves with Arduino/Raspberry Pi and perform necessary software installations. Practical applications include interfacing LED/Buzzer with Arduino, using DHT11 sensors, and establishing Bluetooth communication.

SECTION: Suggested Reading Material
Recommended books include "Internet of Things: A Hands-on Approach" by Vijay Madisetti and Arshdeep Bahga, "Introduction to Internet of Things: A Practical Approach" by Dr. SRN Reddy, Rachit Thukral, and Manasi Mishra, and "Designing the Internet of Things" by Adrian McEwen.
