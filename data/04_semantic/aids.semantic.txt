SECTION: Educational Objectives and Outcomes
Graduates from the B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science program will be prepared for analysing, designing, developing, and testing software solutions and products with creativity and sustainability. Additionally, graduates will be skilled in the use of modern tools for critical problem-solving and analyzing industrial and societal requirements. Furthermore, they will be equipped with managerial and leadership skills for career advancement and starting their own firms.

SECTION: Program Specific Outcomes
Engineering Graduates will be able to develop creative solutions by adapting emerging technologies and tools for real-time applications in industry. They will apply their acquired knowledge to develop software solutions and innovative mobile applications for various automation applications.

SECTION: Program Outcomes
Engineering graduates will exhibit a comprehensive understanding of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and their engineering specialization to solve complex engineering problems. They will identify, formulate, and analyze complex engineering problems to reach substantiated conclusions using principles from mathematics and natural sciences. Graduates will design solutions for such complex problems while considering public health, safety, and environmental factors. They will use research-based knowledge and methods to conduct investigations, analyze data, and synthesize information for valid conclusions. Graduates will demonstrate an ability to create and apply modern engineering tools while recognizing their limitations. They will also assess societal, health, and legal responsibilities relevant to professional engineering practice.
