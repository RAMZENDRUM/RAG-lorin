SECTION: Overview of MSAJCE Alumni Association  
The MSAJCE Alumni Association aims to reconnect and foster an intellectual and emotional relationship between alumni and Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering. This association also promotes voluntary commitment to support future engineers and facilitates an industry-institute relationship.

SECTION: Objectives of the Alumni Association  
The goals are to foster a sense of connectedness back to the institution, establish and renew student relationships, involve alumni in college activities, assist needy students through scholarships, maintain employment databases to aid placements, arrange teaching and training classes for students, and contribute to the overall upgrading of the institution.
