[SECTION: Graduation Day Events]
The 18th Graduation Day at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering is scheduled for February 22, 2025, celebrating the graduation of students from the 2020 to 2024 batch. This significant event also marks the culmination of academic journeys for several other batches, including the 17th Graduation Day on September 21, 2024, for the 2018 to 2022 and 2019 to 2023 cohorts. Key activities during the ceremonies include speeches from distinguished guests and the distribution of degrees, fostering a sense of achievement and community among graduates.

[SECTION: Admission Information]
Admissions are open for the 2026-2027 academic year at Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering, with an online application form available for prospective students. Interested candidates can also access detailed information about fee payments and program offerings through the college’s official website. The institution is recognized for its commitment to providing quality engineering education and comprehensive support for students throughout their academic journey.

[SECTION: Institutional Overview]
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering, located in Siruseri, Chennai, emphasizes a vision centered on excellence in engineering education, guided by its management and principal's message. The college integrates various committees, such as the Grievance and Redressal Committee and the Women's Empowerment Cell, to ensure a supportive academic environment. MSAJCE is committed to fostering innovation and research, making it a distinguished member of the Group of Institutions.

[SECTION: Support Services]
MSAJCE provides extensive support services to enhance student life, including a well-equipped Learning Centre (Library), hostel accommodations, and sports facilities. Through active clubs and societies, students can engage in social services and professional development, contributing to a well-rounded college experience. The institution's emphasis on extracurricular activities supports students' personal growth and community involvement, reflecting its holistic educational approach.
