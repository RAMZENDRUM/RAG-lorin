SECTION: Overview  
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering offers a comprehensive educational platform for students seeking degrees such as B.E, B.Tech, and M.E. The college emphasizes admissions for the academic years 2026-2027 along with various student-centric services, including an Online Application Form and Online Fee Payment options. The college is located at 34, Rajiv Gandhi Salai (OMR), Siruseri IT Park, Siruseri, Chennai - 603103, with a contact number of +91 99400 04500 and an email address of msajce.office@gmail.com.

SECTION: Communication Language Learning Centre Objectives  
The students will be able to acquire knowledge in foreign languages and become proficient in communication skills – Listening, Speaking, Reading, and Writing (LSRW) to achieve employability standards at the international level. The students will become expert bilinguals to learn work ethics and business etiquette in career opportunities. The students will gain deeper knowledge and higher understanding of foreign languages, boost professional value in Business, Social Science, Humanities, Technology, and Science, and get exposure to culture ingrained in the people of various countries. Furthermore, the students will improve higher thinking skills, learn new strategies, and gain exposure to advanced technologies, growing as leaders in many leading sectors to drive for innovation.

SECTION: Networking Academy Partnership  
Mohamed Sathak A. J. College of Engineering has partnered with CISCO, the world leaders in networking, to establish the Networking Academy (NetAcad). This collaboration provides students with a unique hands-on learning experience in the latest networking, security, and cloud technologies using simulation tools. This program offers professional certification recognized by employers worldwide, as well as career pathways to connect to an estimated eight million networking jobs around the world.

SECTION: Courses Offered  
The college offers a variety of courses including Network Essentials, Mobility Fundamentals, CCNA: Introduction to Networks, CCNA: Switching, Routing, and Wireless Essentials, CCNA: Enterprise Networking, Security and Automation or Networking Courses, Security Courses, IoT & Data Analytics Courses, OS and IT Courses, Programming Courses, and Entrepreneurship.

SECTION: Certifications  
Students can earn certifications such as CISCO Certified Network Associate and learn Implementation of CISCO Enterprise Network Core Technologies through the Networking Academy program, enhancing their prospects for employment in various technical fields.
