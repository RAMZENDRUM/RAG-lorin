SECTION: Overview
The Mohamed Sathak A.J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) was established on 5th July 2001 under the aegis of Mohamed Sathak Trust. MSAJCE is approved by AICTE New Delhi and is affiliated to Anna University Chennai and the Government of Tamil Nadu. The campus is located inside the SIPCOT IT Park, Siruseri, spreading over 70 acres of greenery and is surrounded by multinational IT companies such as TCS, CTS, Intellect, Aspire, Steria, Polaris, and FSS.

SECTION: Infrastructure
MSAJCE boasts good infrastructure facilities including spacious classrooms, drawing halls, laboratories, seminar halls, computer facilities with internet and Wi-Fi connectivity, a library, workshops, an auditorium, and a playground. The college is well connected by road, rail, and air from various parts of the country, enhancing accessibility for students and faculty.

SECTION: Faculty and Training
A team of well-qualified and experienced faculty members supports the teaching and learning process at MSAJCE. The college emphasizes equal importance for academic, co-curricular, and extracurricular activities, offering various training programs to ensure the professional and overall transformation of the students.

SECTION: Industrial Collaboration and Opportunities
MSAJCE is located in a green lush environment at Siruseri IT Park, surrounded by over 100 leading IT industries including TCS, CTS, Infosys, and HCL. The institute provides state-of-the-art facilities and industry-collaborative technology centers to facilitate hands-on experience for students.

SECTION: Academic Process and Learning
The college implements a well-defined academic process that encourages smart learning. Students are involved in various research and industrial projects, including industrial hackathons and project competitions, fostering competency-based learning. Professional society and club activities further enable innovative learning among students.

SECTION: Student Development Programs
MSAJCE offers a Student Exchange Program for cultural learning along with internships, industrial visits, guest lectures, and seminars/webinars conducted by industrial experts to meet industrial expectations. A Communication Language Learning facility is available offering courses in English, German, and Japanese.

SECTION: Value Added Education
The college provides value-added courses and technology training to bridge the gap between the institute and industry, aiming for a good placement record. MSAJCE also supports students in pursuing higher education or becoming entrepreneurs.
