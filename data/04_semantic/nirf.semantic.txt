SECTION: National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2026
The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2026 categorized its rankings into three distinct sections: NIRF 2026 – Engineering, NIRF 2026 – Overall, and NIRF 2026 – Innovation.

SECTION: National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2025
NIRF 2025 also included three categories: NIRF 2025 – Engineering, NIRF 2025 – Overall, and NIRF 2025 – Innovation.

SECTION: National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2024
For NIRF 2024, the rankings were structured into two categories: NIRF 2024 – Engineering and NIRF 2024 – Overall.

SECTION: National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2023
Lastly, the NIRF 2023 presented rankings in two areas: NIRF 2023 – Engineering and NIRF 2023 – Overall.
