SECTION: Research Objectives
The Research and Development Cell aims to nurture research culture in the College by promoting research in newly emerging and challenging areas of Engineering, Technology, Science, and Humanities. 
To encourage students and faculty members to carry out research in the fields of Engineering, Technology, Science, and Humanities by providing necessary facilities and infrastructures required for carrying out research. 
To create zeal among the students and faculty towards research and innovation and to encourage them to publish their findings in conferences and journals. 
To assist students to apply for funding for conducting research under the student project scheme to various funding agencies such as TNSCST, MSME, IEI (I) etc. 
To work closely with the industry to understand their needs, which will result in new or improved products, processes, systems, or services that can increase the company's productivity and promote Institute-Industry Collaboration. 
To support the government in serving society by taking up sponsored projects offered by agencies such as TNSCST, DST, AICTE, UGC, ISRO, DRDO, CSIR, DBT etc. 
To enhance the general research capability of budding engineers by participating in conferences, seminars, workshops, and project competitions. 
To organize various workshops, seminars, and trainings related to the promotion of research. 
To encourage both students and faculty to apply for copyrights and patents.
