Industry Institution Interaction Cell
Technical Institutes have a closer relationship with society since they were more concerned with the applied aspects of science and technology, to solve real-world problems, but always there is a gap between Industry and Institute. In a competitive market due to increasing globalization of business, to and bridge the gap between the industry and institute is inevitable, hence now a day’s industries and institutes seeks to use strategies, tools and techniques jointly to increase efficiency and effectiveness of deployable engineers at the same time maximizing quality and minimizing cost. The MSAJCE understands that better interaction between technical institutions and industry is very essential. So MSAJCE is creating and utilizing the opportunity through III Cell to collaborate with industries to fulfilling the needs in terms that are mutually acceptable and beneficial way. MSAJCE-III Cell plays an important role to make the student as well as faculty to understand present industrial expectations and relevant skill sets required for students. This makes the platform for the students to be aware of the gaps in their skills and provides an opportunity to upgrade them. It also enables the faculty to train students to meet the industrial expectations and needs.
