Mechanical Engineering

Admission through TNEA Counselling

Students who have completed Plus 2 / Diploma may apply for Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissoins (TNEA Counselling) for B.E / B.Tech programmes admission conducted by Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE), Chennai.

For More Details Please Visit    TNEA     DoTE      Anna University

Eligibility for Students from Other States

(Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Kerala and all Northern States):

Selection of candidates will be basd on the qualifying marks (Q) obtained in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry at the Puls Two or Intermediate level. The qualifying marks (Q) are calculated as follows:

Q = (Mathematics / 2) + ((Physics + Chemistry) / 4),

where M.P. and C represent the percentage marks in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry, respectively.

B.E / B.Tech - Admission

Eligibility

B.E. / B.Tech. Degree Programmes (4 Years)

HSC (Academic)

A pass in the HSC (Academic) or its equivalent with a minimum average percentage in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry put together as given below:

 

S.No. Community A Pass with Minimum average marks in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry put together
1 General Category 45.00%
2 Backward Class including Backward Class Muslim 40.00%
3 MBC & DNC 40.00%
4 SC / SCA / ST 40.00%
Qualifying Exam - HSC (Vocational)

A pass in any one of the HSC (Vocational subject) with any one of the Engineering related subjects namely Mathematics, Physics or Chemistry with a minimum average percentage put together as given below:

 

S.No. Community A Pass with Minimum average marks in related subjects, Vocational Theory and Practicals put together
1 General Category 45.00%
2 Backward Class including Backward Class Muslim 40.00%
3 MBC & DNC 40.00%
4 SC / SCA / ST 40.00%

Direct Second Year B.E./B.Tech. (Lateral Entry) Degree Courses

Qualifying Examination:

Diploma Candidates :

A pass with prescribed minimum eligibility marks in any one of the Diploma in appropriate branch of Engineering / Technology of the State Board of Technical Education and Training, Tamil Nadu / equivalent prescribed for admission to the Degree Course (Refer list of eligible Degree Courses for various Diploma Courses)

B.Sc. Candidates :

A pass with prescribed minimum eligibility marks in any recognized Bachelor’s Degree in Science of minimum 3 years duration with 10+2+3 years pattern and studied core Mathematics as one of the subjects at degree level
Note:Candidates with Degree other than Bachelor of Science are not eligible.

For any clarification with regard to Diploma qualification, write a letter to DOTE, get it clarified and then admit students. On any account, candidates without prescribed qualifications will not be given admission approval.

Minimum Eligibility Marks:

Both Diploma and B.Sc. qualified candidates have to obtain the following minimum eligibility marks for admission to Lateral Entry as prescribed in G.O. 4 SC / SCA / ST Mere pass in the qualifying examination for Diploma examinations as stipulated by the

Ph.D - Mechanical Engineering - Admission

Eligibility

          1.1 Master's Degree or any other qualification recognized as equivalent thereto in the fields of study notified from time to time by Anna University. Specific educational qualifications are given below :

 

S.No. Programme Qualification for Admission
1 Ph.D Degree in Mechanical Engineering M.E./M.Tech./M.S. (By Research) in the relevant branch of Engineering or Technology

Note : Candidates should have the required qualification in the order of studies 10th, HSC, UG/PG degree or its equivalent qualification.

          1.2 Bachelor's Degree in Engineering / Technology of the University or any other qualification recognized as equivalent thereto in the fields of study with a minimum of 15 years of R & D Experience in National Research Laboratories / Public sector undertakings and with minimum of three publications in referred impact factor journals or two approved international patents in the last five years.

          1.3 A minimum of 55% marks or CGPA of 5.5 on a 10 point scale in the qualifying examination (In case of SC/ST/ Differently –Abled candidates, 50% marks or CGPA of 5.0 on a 10 point scale).
