Overview

Alumni association of Mohamed Sathak AJ College of Engg welcomes you all to join us.This association is formed to reconnect and foster intellectual and emotional relationship between alumni and parent organisation. Also bring in a good will and voluntary commitment to support future engineers of the institution and provide industry institute relationship and enthusiastically participate in various activities organised by the college

MSAJCE Alumnus Strength in Social Media Platform

Our Objectives

To foster a sense of connectedness back to the institution
To establish and renew student relationship
To involve in various activities of the college
To provide assistance to needful students by way of funds and scholarship
To gather and maintain database of employment and assist in placements
To arrange teaching and training classes to students
To contribute for the overall upgrading of the institution
