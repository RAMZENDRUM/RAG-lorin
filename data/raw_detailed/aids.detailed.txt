AI is a term used to describe the simulation of human intelligence processes in machines. It typically involves sophisticated algorithms that enable computers to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition and decision making etc,. Data Science is a field of knowledge which applies advanced statistical and mathematical techniques to large datasets in order to extract knowledge from them. This data can be structured like a spreadsheet but can also be unstructured like text or video data. Data science is the application of techniques from statistics, mathematics, computer programming and other fields to solve real-world problems using information technology. Due to the exploration of the Internet, AI and DS deliver data-driven solutions using computational principles, methods, and systems for extracting information from data and modern computational systems that reveal capabilities of insight, way of thinking, learning, and action that are typical of human intelligence. B.Tech Artificial Intelligence and Data Science ( AIDS ) has emerged as one of the most high-growth, dynamic, and lucrative careers in technology, Since 2015. This course aims at providing not only the core technologies such as artificial intelligence, data mining, and data modeling but also gives intensive inputs in areas of machine learning and big data analytics. Through this course, the students will gain cross-disciplinary skills across fields such as statistics, computer science, machine learning, and logic, data scientists and may have career opportunities in healthcare, business, e-commerce, social networking companies, climatology, biotechnology, genetics, and other important areas. The major focus of this programme is to equip students with statistical, mathematical reasoning, machine learning, knowledge discovery, and visualization skills. B.Tech Artificial Intelligence and Data Science ( AIDS ) is a specialized branch that deals with the core principles and ethical issues in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence and motivates students to explore the cutting-edge applications related to Big Data, Neural Networks, and Deep Learning. Applications like Alexa / Siri, Erica / Sophia, Tesla’s driverless car, Chat-bot, etc. are becoming common. AI-powered Robots can take over human life-endangering jobs like firefighting, deep-sea oil drilling, mining works with high mortality rates, etc. The Career Opportunity for AIDS graduates at various sectors such as Manufacturing, Software, Research, Education, Defense, Banking & Finance, e-Commerce, transport and Health Science are as follows • Software Engineer • Software Tester • Application Developer • Information and Multimedia Designer • Medical Information Scientist • Technology Developer • IT Specialist • Technical Consultant • Quality Analyst • Information Network Manager • Data Quality Analyst Programme B.Tech. - Artificial Intelligence And Data Science Duration: 4 years (Regular) / 3 years (Lateral Entry) No. of Semesters: 8 (Regular) / 6 (Lateral Entry) Intake / No. of Seats: Total - 30 (Government - 15, Management - 15) Eligibility: 10+2 system of Education. Must have secured a pass in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in the qualifying examination. Job Profiles AI and Data Science Growth in India by 2025 – 2030 in India The Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science sectors in India are having an explosive growth over the next five years. AI-driven solutions and rely on data analytics to make better decisions, the demand for professionals in these fields is expected to grow significantly. The employment opportunity forecast for AI and Data Science in India from 2025 to 2030 is as follows. * Key Drivers: Increased AI Adoption Across Industries: Healthcare, Finance, E-commerce, Telecommunications, Automotive, and Manufacturing sectors. AI will be used for tasks like Predictive analytics, Personalized services, Data-driven decision-making, and Automation. AI-driven solutions in core business functions (HR, finance, customer service) Government Support and Initiatives: Digital India, National AI Strategy, and the National AI Mission initiatives boost AI research, startups, and adoption across public services. Global leader in AI for sectors such as Smart cities, Healthcare, and Agriculture. Rising Demand for Data-Driven Decision Making: Customer segmentation, Fraud detection, and Supply chain optimization. Growth in AI-Powered Startups: AI startups is expected to double in the next few years, creating job opportunities for AI engineers, data scientists, and product managers. Integration of AI into Business Functions: Industries like finance, retail, e-commerce, and manufacturing are increasingly adopting AI-based automation to streamline operations, reduce costs, and offer personalized experiences to customers. Key AI and Data Science Job Roles in India (2025-2030) Data Scientist: Roles: Analyze large data sets to derive actionable insights, build predictive models, And help organizations make data-driven decisions. Skills: Python, R, SQL, Machine Learning Algorithms, Statistical analysis, Data visualization (Tableau, Power BI), Deep learning. Demand: The demand for data scientists in India will remain high, with industries across healthcare, finance, retail, and e-commerce Machine Learning Engineer: Roles: Build and deploy machine learning models, implement algorithms, and optimize the model performance for real-world applications. Skills: Python, TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch, Neural networks, Cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud), Algorithm optimization. Demand: As companies adopt more AI solutions, the demand for ML engineers will rise, especially in startups and tech giants. AI Research Scientist: Roles: Conduct cutting-edge research in AI, focus on Deep learning, Reinforcement learning, Computer vision, and Natural language processing (NLP). Skills: Machine learning algorithms, Python, Mathematical modeling, Research publications, Deep learning frameworks. Demand: With growing AI investment in India, especially from global tech companies and research labs, there will be a high demand for AI research scientists. Data Analyst: Roles: Collect, process, and analyze data to uncover trends, create dashboards, and support decision-making with data-driven insights. Skills: Excel, SQL, Python, data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI), statistical tools (R, SAS). Demand: As companies focus more on data-driven decision-making, the demand for data analysts in sectors like finance, retail, and healthcare will increase. Data Engineer: Roles: Design, construct, and maintain large-scale data pipelines, work with big data technologies to ensure data is stored, processed, and accessible. Skills: SQL, Python, Hadoop, Spark, Kafka, cloud services (AWS, Google Cloud), ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes. Demand: With the growth of big data and cloud computing, the demand for data engineers will continue to rise, especially in data-driven companies. AI Product Manager: Roles: Oversee the development and deployment of AI-powered products, manage cross-functional teams of engineers and data scientists. Skills: Product lifecycle management, agile methodology, machine learning, project management, business strategy. Demand: The rise in AI product development across industries will create more opportunities for AI product managers. Business Intelligence (BI) Developer: Roles: Design and develop BI tools to collect, store, and analyze business data, help businesses make strategic decisions using AI-driven insights. Skills: SQL, Power BI, Tableau, data visualization, reporting tools, Python. Demand: As companies shift to data-driven strategies, the demand for BI developers will increase, particularly in e-commerce and retail. Natural Language Processing (NLP) Engineer Roles: Work on algorithms and models that allow machines to process and understand human language, like chatbots, virtual assistants, and sentiment analysis. Skills: Python, NLP frameworks (NLTK, SpaCy, Hugging Face), machine learning, deep learning, text mining. Demand: With the rise of AI chatbots, voice assistants, and text analytics, demand for NLP engineers will grow in e-commerce, finance, and telecommunications. Banks, Insurance companies, and Fintech startups. E-Commerce and Retail: Personalization, Dynamic pricing, Inventory optimization, and Recommendation systems. Automotive and Autonomous Vehicles: Autonomous driving, Vehicle-to-everything (V2X) communication, and Predictive maintenance, Computer vision, Sensor data processing, and AI-powered automotive solution. Telecommunication Sector: Network optimization, Customer service Chatbots, and Predictive maintenance, 5G, IoT, and Edge computing.
